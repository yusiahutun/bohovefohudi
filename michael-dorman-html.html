    New Zealand actor who came into the limelight thanks to his roles on the series Patriot and Wonderland. His fame escalated after landing parts on the shows The Secret Life of Us and For All Mankind.

    Before Fame

    He studied at QUT Gardens Point Campus in Brisbane, Australia.

    Trivia

    He earned a 2003 Logie Award nomination for Most Popular New Male Talent.

    Family Life

    He married Tessa Richardson in 2011 but they divorced in 2019.

    Associated With

    He appeared in the 2017 film Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales which starred Johnny Depp.
