    "In 2009 my life completely changed courses when my then-boyfriend told me he was thinking about joining the military. We were high-school sweethearts and the thought of him moving a thousand miles away while I finished my senior year was devastating. In July 2009, he went off to basic training in Texas and we spent the next year living states away from each other. But we made our relationship work through Skype and visits whenever he had a free weekend. In October 2010, just four months after my high-school graduation, we got married and I packed up my life and followed him down to North Carolina. We clung to each other and enjoyed our lives as newlyweds. Then in May 2012, we were blessed with a beautiful baby girl we named Arya. Once again, our lives completely changed. We went from it being just the two of us, to being a family of three, and we were so filled with joy.

    I knew I wanted to have newborn pictures taken of our daughter, so we could look back and cherish how little she once was. Since the Air Force was such a big part of our lives, I knew I wanted to get some photos with Matt in his uniform. The photographer suggested trying this pose, and I'm so glad she did, because this picture is one of my favorites that I will treasure forever. I was a little surprised when a friend of ours told us our daughter's picture was on Pinterest and Instagram. When I clicked on it, I couldn't believe how many times it was repinned and liked! It's pretty crazy to see a photo of your husband and daughter shared around social media, but it's also pretty cool that other people see our photo as beautiful and an inspiration for their own photo shoots.

    Our daughter is three-years-old now and is such a smart and funny little girl. We have since moved to New York and we now also have a beautiful ten-month-old son. The past seven years have been a crazy ride, filled with so many wonderful moments, and I know there are so many more to come. I am so very thankful that I have been blessed with such an amazing little family to share these amazing moments with." â€”Hollie L.
