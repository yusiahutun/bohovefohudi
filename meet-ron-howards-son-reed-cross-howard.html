    Many people become famous from birth because of their parents and family. For Reed Cross Howard, his popularity is because of his parents. Reed is a 36-year-old American professional golfer and the only son of famous television and film actor and award-winning director Ron Howard.

    Like many celebrity kids, the successful athlete once appeared in a film directed by his father. However, he did not make a career in showbiz like the rest of his family; his true passion is golfing.

    Reed Cross Howard’s Early Life

    Reed Cross Howard was born in the United States of America to his parents, Ron Howard and Cheryl Howard, in 1987. The famous director’s son has three elder sisters, Bryce Dallas Howard, Paige Howard, and Jocelyn Howard.

    Hence, he is the youngest child of the family. He grew up with his parents and siblings in the New York City suburb of Banksville. His father is a famous American filmmaker and director, while his mother is an actress who appeared in several films directed by her husband.

    Is Ron Howard’s Son an Actor?

    While the entire family is into entertainment, Reed Cross Howard chose a different career path. Like his parents and siblings, the professional golfer graced the screen in an episode of a comedy series, “Arrested Development,” directed by his father. However, lights, camera, and action weren’t his thing. Ron Howard’s son is not an actor; he has always been interested in sports and pursued his passion.

    Is Reed Cross Howard a Professional Golf Player?

    The successful athlete is a professional golf player. Although Reed has not played in the big leagues, he is well on the path. He began his career in 2007 at the University of Southern California (USCB), where he doubled as a student and athlete.

    He played on the school’s team till 2008, after which he competed in the U.S Amateur Championship due to his jaw-dropping talent. Reed Cross Howard has won numerous golfing tournaments since then. 

    However, Ron Howard’s son has asserted he is a professional golfer. In addition to this, the athlete has a Youtube channel where he creates tutorials for amateur golfers and trains them virtually. He provides Steadhead training aids to assist amateur golfers in playing more accurate shots. 

    Is Reed Cross Howard Married?

    The successful golfer is a celebrity kid known to guard the details of his personal life. The golf player kept his dating life out of the media. No one knew anything about his relationship status until 2015 his celebrity father spilled the beans.

    In 2015 Ron took to his Facebook account to share a wedding picture of Reed and his bride, who was immediately identified by fans as Ashley Gioaffre. The announcement surprised fans, but they were happy for the newly wedded couple.

    Is Reed Cross Howard on Social Media?

    Reed Cross Howard has a social media presence, like many celebrity kids. The golfer is active on Instagram, and he has over 8k followers.

    He also has a Youtube Channel, “Reed Howard Golf,” where he live-streams and uploads golf training videos. His live streams and tutorials aim to help amateur golfers perfect their techniques. His Youtube Channel currently has over 1.11k subscribers.

    How Many Kids Does Ron Howard’s Son Have?

    Reed Howard and Ashley Gioaffre are proud parents of two. The pair welcomed their first child barely four years into their marriage, a beautiful daughter named Aspen Howard. Like his wedding, Reed’s father took to his Facebook to announce the arrival of his granddaughter. Fans reacted to the good news by leaving several congratulatory comments. 

    The athlete and his wife have a second daughter, but the details of her birth and her name are also unknown. The couple wants to keep the identity of their children private.

    Although he is rarely seen in the limelight, the professional golfer enjoys fatherhood to the fullest. Reed couldn’t look any happier in pictures with his first daughter, Aspen when he took to his Instagram to celebrate her first birthday. Reed disclosed in a 2019 interview that he believes that “a dad’s presence emphasizes the impact a father can have on his children.” 

    The successful golfer is entirely into being a dad and tries to be a role model to his girls, just like famous R&B singer Chris Brown.
