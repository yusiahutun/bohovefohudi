    Christmas is on the horizon, and that means the return of all of our favorite activities. We're talking about baking cookies, decorating gingerbread houses, and watching Christmas movies. But it also means the return on one of our favorite traditions: The Elf on the Shelf. The official Elf Return Week is November 22-December 1. Whether you're new to the Elf game or are adding another Scout Elf to your family, you need to take care of one important piece of business first: Choosing an Elf on the Shelf name.

    It's the first rule of being an Elf owner. Once your Elf on the Shelf arrives, you must pick a name. Here, we've rounded up the absolute best Elf on the Shelf names so that you can give your Elf a name that's as mischievous, adorable, and fun as he or she is. After all, elves, like humans, are picky when it comes to their own names, and they're not willing to settle for just anything. You've got to get really creative (as creative as you are with your other Elf on the Shelf ideas and Elf on the Shelf return plans!), think outside the box, and select a name that fits the personality of your family's particular Elf.

    Luckily, our extensive list of Elf on the Shelf names for boys and girls is here to give you a bit of Christmas inspiration. Grab a notebook, start jotting down your favorites, and get ready to choose the best Elf name for your little troublemaker. You may love some of these names so much, you'll want to buy multiple Elves!
