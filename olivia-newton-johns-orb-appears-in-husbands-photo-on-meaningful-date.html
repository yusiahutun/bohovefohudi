    Olivia Newton-John’s husband and daughter reveal they’ve both had ‘supernatural’ orb encounters following the star’s tragic death last year.

    The beloved movie star sadly died aged 73 after a long battle with breast cancer. Fans across the world mourned the loss of the Grease actor who had been a trailblazer in cancer awareness since her diagnosis decades earlier. Now, as the first anniversary of the Hollywood icon’s death passes by, her daughter revealed she has had supernatural encounters since the film world lost a legend.

    Olivia Newton-John’s daughter found ‘orb’ in photo after death

    “Two weeks after she passed, my phone accidentally took a picture of my dog, and there floating by his head was a little blue orb,” daughter Chloe Lattanzi said.

    She told People it was the same color as the necklace she was wearing which her stepdad, John Easterling, gave Olivia – who later handed it down to Chloe before her death.

    But her late husband has also seen the same thing. John held a private ceremony in Peru to spread Olivia’s ashes at the exact spot they exchanged vows. He took on the trip on what would have been the couple’s 15th wedding anniversary.

    He said: “I took a picture, and this blue orb is right between my eyes… It’s been a supernatural year”.

    They both believe the two photos are supernatural encounters with Olivia.

    ‘There’s no replacing her’

    Olivia Newton-John sadly died at her home in California after battling breast cancer for 30 years. The celebrity icon was first diagnosed back in 1992, and then again 20 years later in 2013 which she kept private. However, four years later, it came back a third time in 2017, telling fans the disease had metastasized to her sacrum.

    Looking back at the past 12 months without her, John admitted, “there’s no replacing her”. Fans across the world feel the same for the compassionate advocate.

    Chloe and John are working together to lead the annual Olivia’s Walk for Wellness in Melbourne – native Australian Olivia’s homeland. The initiative raises much-needed funds for life-saving cancer research.

    Olivia’s final days as daughter moved back home to care for her

    Chloe also spoke about the music star’s final days at home, after they agreed to take her out of the hospital because she “didn’t want to be there”.

    Her final words were “I love you” as she looked over at her daughter by her bedside.

    Since then, she has said it was a “very special” moment for her. They also “feel her every single day”.

    Following Olivia’s death, tributes poured in from fellow actors and fans.

    Grease co-star John Travolta choked up on stage as he honored her at the 2023 Oscars award show. He can be seen visibly getting emotional in a Twitter clip. The Pulp Fiction star fights back tears while he remembered his ‘Sandy’.
