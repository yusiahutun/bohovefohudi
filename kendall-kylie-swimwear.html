    Victoria’s Secret may be axing its swimwear line, but Kendall and Kylie Jenner just announced that they’re releasing a swim collaboration with Topshop, so it all evens out. In a neon green bikini emblazoned with “Kendall + Kylie” at top and bottom, Kylie broadcast the news from her Instagram today, and the crowd went wild (25,000 comments and counting). Kendall is too cool to put this on her own Insta, but she did post a couple of Polaroids (so hip) on their brand’s IG. These shots show her posed in a tasteful white two-piece, with a beachy L.A. vibe going on in the background.

    While IG is cool and all, the sisters really went to town on their Snapchats today, posting tons of snaps and videos live from the photo shoot of their new venture.
