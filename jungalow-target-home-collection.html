    Spring has officially sprung! Okay, maybe it’s about to be 10 degrees across most of the northeast this weekend, but that doesn’t mean we can’t look ahead to the future and wish for warmer days. Thankfully, the Opalhouse spring collection has dropped at Target with new products, so if you can’t experience a colorful and cheery world quite yet, you can at least shop for it.

    If you’re confused, Jungalow is a lifestyle brand by Justina Blakeney and Opalhouse is Target’s exclusive home line. Together, they’re able to bring more fall decor offerings at affordable price points. The spring 2023 drop includes stylish outdoor furniture, bright throw pillows, unique home decor items and more. Personally, I’m eyeing all of the super cute bathroom decor that just dropped. I think my bathroom could use a spring refresh after the long and dreary winter.

    Related Stories

    Get ready to invite your friends over for lemonade on the terrace and show off all of the new Target finds around your home. With this collection, you better believe your friends are going to be asking you where you’ve been shopping. 

    Here are our top picks for spring decor in the new Jungalow x Opalhouse drop, and you better believe we’ll be updating the list as the season continues.

    Our mission at STYLECASTER is to bring style to the people, and we only feature products we think you’ll love as much as we do. Target is a STYLECASTER sponsor, however, all products in this article were independently selected by our editors.     Okay, I don’t want to call this the cutest pillow I’ve ever seen but I kind of want to call it the cutest pillow I’ve ever seen. I mean, who else is going to have a lizard shaped throw pillow on their couch? I love that the Opalhouse line lets you stand out without paying big bucks. It also comes in blue!

    Molise Pouf

    This pouf is the perfect addition to any bedroom or living room and doubles as an extra seat when guests are around. When they’re not, put a flat tray on top and store books, a water carafe or whatever inspires you!
