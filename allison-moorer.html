    Who is Allison Moorer?

    Allison Moorer is a well-known American singer-songwriter. In 1997, she started working with MCA Nashville, and in collaboration with Gwil Owen, she co-wrote the blockbuster hit song “A Soft Place To Fall,” which debuted on the Billboard Country Chart.

    For her ten albums, Trisha Yearwood, Kenny Chesney, Miranda Lambert, Steve Earle, and Hayes Carll all contributed songs. Her debut studio album, Alabama Song, was released in 1998.
