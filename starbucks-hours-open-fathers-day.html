    Father’s Day, June 19 this year, is fast approaching, which means ways to celebrate might be top of mind right about now. Perhaps you’re brainstorming the best Father's Day gift for your dad, step-dad, or grand-dad. Or maybe you’re considering skipping a store-bought present in favor of a DIY gift idea the dad in your life will actually use. And then there’s the chance that you’re looking beyond tangible gifts and planning a series of Father’s Day activities to enjoy with your dad. Whatever way you plan to celebrate the day, we have a hunch that you’ll need a little extra energy to make it all happen.

    Fortunately, there's Starbucks. Whether you need to stop in for a quick grande blonde roast to power through the day or are hoping to treat the dad in your life to a cup, Starbucks is here to help. If you resonate with the latter, you’ll be glad to know the store offers more than just pre-made coffee. You can choose from sleek stainless travel mugs, glass coffee cups, yummy treats, and more. And, if you’re not quite sure what to get your dad, but know that he loves coffee, you can never go wrong with a Starbucks gift card.

    Of course, if you’ve been a busy bee leading up to the big day, you might have one pressing question sitting at the forefront of your mind.

    Is Starbucks Open on Father’s Day 2022?

    Thankfully, the fan-favorite coffee chain understands the need for a cuppa Joe on Father’s Day (and every day), so they remain open on holidays for all to enjoy. Hours vary by location, so your best bet is to check your local store hours to see when they're open so you don't waste a trip!
