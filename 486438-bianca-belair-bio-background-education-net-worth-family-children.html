    Bianca Belair is an American wrestler and former track and field athlete. She is well known for winning the WWE Raw Women’s Championship during Wrestlemania 38. In 2021, Bianca made history by winning the 2021 Women’s Royal Rumble match.

    She became the second African-American wrestler to win a Royal Rumble after Dwayne’ The Rock’ Johnson. But perhaps people recognise her even more for being the wife of fellow wrestler Montez Ford.

    Early life

    The wrestler was born Bianca Nicole Blair on 9 April 1989 (age 33 as of 2022) in Knoxville, Tennessee, United States. She is the daughter of Leonard Blair (father) and Travonda Blair (mother). Furthermore, Bianca has four siblings, Jeffery, Leonard Jr., Jermaine, and Jasta.

    Bianca Belair’s family did not have much while growing up. Nevertheless, her parents helped her enrol in a gymnastics class before she was five.     Bianca attended Austin-East Magnet High School, a public high school in Knoxville, Tennessee. She continued her activities as an athlete, mostly performing on track and field. As a result, Bianca won many races, including hurdles.

    Professional career

    After graduating from the University of Tennessee, Bianca Belair joined CrossFit as a competitor and powerlifter. Unfortunately, her career at CrossFit ended prematurely after an injury led her doctors to diagnose her with slipping rib syndrome, where the lower ribs move a little more than normal, causing pain. But she was lucky enough to appear in several magazines, including Femme Rouge and RX.

    Bianca moved on from CrossFit and worked as a phone salesperson at a flavouring company in Atlanta. Then, in 2016, she applied for WWE prospects and was lucky to get a trial. Finally, on 12 April 2016, she signed a contract with WWE. WWE assigned her to the WWE Performance Center in Orlando, Florida.

    She then debuted in an in-ring segment at NXT live event on 25 June 2016. However, her professional match as a wrestler started on 29 September 2016 with a loss. Belair competed in NXT between 2016 and 2019. Her last match was on 19 February 2019, when she lost to Charlotte Flair.

    Bianca Belair made the Raw Roster in 2020. After that, WWE assigned her to the SmackDown roster. On 31 January 2021, Bianca eliminated Rhea Ripley to win a Royal Rumble match. And on 10 April 2021, Bianca won the WWE SmackDown Women’s Championship, the first title of her wrestling career.

    On 1 October 2021, WWE drafted Belair back to Raw. Bianca then fought to become the number one contender for the Raw Women’s Championship at WrestleMania 38. And when the event began on 2 April 2022, she defeated Becky Lynch to win the Raw Women’s Championship. So as of December 2022, she remains the reigning WWE Raw Women’s champion.
