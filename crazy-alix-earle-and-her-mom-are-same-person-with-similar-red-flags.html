    Alix Earle has been stunning her fans at event after event in 2023. The influencer attended the ESPY Awards alongside her boyfriend, Braxton Berrios, this July. Alix is often sharing many parts of her life with her fans via social media, but one thing people want to know more about is who her parents are.

    TikTok star Alix spends her days creating content, and by the looks of her accounts, she’s having a great time. The blond bombshell rakes in 5.5 million TikTok followers and 2.9 million on Instagram. Alix Earle writes in her bio that she’s a “hot mess,” but she appears to have it all the way together from her GRWM videos. Her mom and dad sometimes feature in her posts, as well as her siblings.

    Meet Alix Earle’s parents

    Influencer Alix Earle was born to Thomas ‘TJ’ and Alissa Earle on December 16, 2000.

    The 22-year-old hails from New Jersey and is currently dating American football player Braxton Berrios.

    Alix’s parents aren’t together nowadays. Her dad, 50, is now married to Ashley Dupre, 38.

    The Sun reports that in 2012, Alix’s mom and dad had been divorced over a year and that he married Ashley in 2013.

    Alix Earle’s mom is her ‘twin’

    Taking to TikTok in July, Alix posted on a Get Ready With Me video alongside her sister, Ashtin.

    Her mom appeared in the TikTok as the ladies got ready for a night out in Wisconsin.

    When her mom showed up in shot wearing a red dress, Alix said she looked “fancy.”

    She added: “Me, my mom, and my grandma are all the same person.”

    Fans were calling Alix’s mom a “party animal,” as she was eager to head to bars in Milwaukee. The TikTok star added that her mom was “begging” them to go to out.

    She continued: “Also, my grandma wants to make a TikTok appearance.”

    Another TikTok from June saw Alix ask her ‘twin’ mom what her “red flags” are.

    Alissa responded: “Your red flags are impatience, you are a slob, crazy…”

    Someone else behind the camera joked: “The apple doesn’t fall far ladies,” as Alix asked where she’d get those traits from.

    TikTok star’s dad is a businessman

    Alix Earle’s parents met at Lynn University, reports The Sun. However, her dad, TJ, is now in a relationship with her step-mom named Ashley.

    TJ is the owner of a construction and asphalt company named Earle. The business has been in the family for over 50 years. He writes on LinkedIn that he’s “…the Treasurer and former three-term President of the New Jersey Asphalt Pavement Association.”

    Alix, her dad and her step-mom often appear on TikToks together as she spends time with her family.

    Many of her fans call for the Earle family to start a reality show. Some write in the comments section: “We NEED a keeping up with the earles.”

    Another commented that her dad “cracks them up.”
