    Proposal In Italy

    Ralph popped the question to Ali in Italy. They were in Tuscany attending the wedding of Andy Serkis and Lorraine Ashbourne. While there, his friend Chris motivated him to propose to his long term lover. His friend Chris even pulled the engagement ring off his wife and gave it to the actor.
