    Green Tomato Fritters Recipe

    Yields: 8 serving(s)

    Prep Time: 15 mins

    Total Time: 40 mins

    Cal/Serv: 220

    Ingredients

    Save Recipe
    • 1 lb.

      green tomatoes (about 4), chopped into matchsticks

    • 1 c.

      chickpea flour

    • 1/2 c.

      rice flour

    • 3

      shallots, thinly sliced

    • 1 tsp.

      cumin seeds

    • 1 tsp.

      chili powder

    • 1 tsp.

      turmeric powder

    • Kosher salt

    • 3 c.

      vegetable oil, for frying

    Directions

    • Step 1In a large bowl, combine first 7 ingredients plus 2 teaspoons salt. Divide mixture into 16 patties, using your palm to gently flatten.
    • Step 2In a heavy 2-quart pot fitted with a deep-fry thermometer, heat oil over medium-high heat to 350Â°F. Working in batches of 4, fry fritters until golden, 3 to 4 minutes. Transfer to a paper-towel-lined plate to drain.
