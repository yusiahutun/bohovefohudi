    UFC star Jessica Eye has hit back at critics of her OnlyFans page after she announced its opening.

    The fighter recently began the premium subscription service and warned viewers of it's 'adult-only content'. She initially posted a picture of herself wearing underwear, saying: "Hey everyone, I’m super excited about my new adult ONLY content page. Click the link in the BIO to join now. "@embraceboudoirohio. "#jessicaevileye #jessicaeye #beautiful #embracethejourney #embraceyourself #nofilterneeded #boudoir #UFC #noshame."

    The update received huge attention, with over 5,000 likes. But it appears along the way, Eye turned off the comments. It came after she was on the receiving end of a lot of negative feedback following her decision to make the move to OnlyFans.

    So much so that Eye, who returns to the ring at UFC 276 on Saturday night against Maycee Barber, took to Twitter to rage at those who criticised her. She blasted: "People will have a opinion no matter what the truth is."

    The 35-year-old is all about being your true self and this is reflective in a post she recently uploaded on Instagram, where she spoke about how she has adopted body positivity.
