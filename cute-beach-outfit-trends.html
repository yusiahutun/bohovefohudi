    Summer is just around the corner which means that if you're lucky, a holiday somewhere gorgeous and warm will be imminent.  

    Generous scoops of gelato, picturesque skylines and frothy seafronts are all within touching distance – only first you need to refine your holiday wish list and organise your packing situation.

    READ: The 6 most stylish belted swimwear pieces to wear this summer

    Dressing for the beach seems simple enough, but why settle for last year's coverup when there are a myriad of stylish options out there right now. In the way of some much-needed beach outfit inspo, we've turned to some of the celebrity looks that have caught our attention recently.

    Hello! Fashion shares our favourite celebrity beach outfits and the pieces you need to recreate them: 

    The all-white ensemble 

    It-girl Sofia Richie championed SS23's most nostalgic trend on her honeymoon by opting for 3D florals by Magda Butrym.

    The splash of colour 

    Global star Dua Lipa made a strong case for orange in an assortment of knitted pieces by Jacquemus, and topped things off with a pair of the label's almond open toe sandals.

    READ: 9 holiday dresses that are perfect for SS23

    MORE: The 17 best designer beach bags for summer 2023

    The slouchy shirt

    Supermodel Adut Akech looked stylish by her sun lounger in a black bikini by I.AM.GIA teamed with a relaxed-fit shirt and a playful bucket hat.

    The frill-adorned bikini 

    Ex-Victoria's Secret model Elsa Hosk gave us major swimwear envy with her frilly floral Heavy Manners bikini. 

    The jaunty cowboy hat

    Emily Ratajkowski was serving major Western vibes in a cute cowboy hat - and now we want one...

    The white halter dress

    Bella Hadid's summer wardrobe is the gift that keeps on giving, and this white halter dress would look killer with sun-kissed limbs.

    The tie-dye bikini  

    Hailey Bieber looked radiant in a triangle-shaped tie-dye bikini styled with a gold medallion-style necklace. The 25-year-old supermodel turned to the 1960s fabric dyeing trend for a bold swimwear moment and we're 100% on board.

    The mesh midi

    Kendall Jenner is a huge fan of the summer mesh dress and opted for a psychedelic print piece from the Jean Paul Gaultier archive. Her vintage halter neck dress was from the label's spring/summer 2000 collection, which she layered over a matching crop top and micro shorts from the same line.
