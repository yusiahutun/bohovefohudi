    Kim Kardashian has a new 'do!

    The "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" star traded in her long brunette locks for a shorter 'do and added dirty blonde ombre highlights.

    She debuted her mane makeover on SnapChat on Wednesday night, posting a video from her "midnight haircut" with celebrity hairstylist Cesar Ramirez, the man behind Khloe Kardashian's blonde hue!

    The mother-of-two followed that up with a series of selfies that showcased her new look from every angle.

    Kim's mommy makeover is just the latest step in her post-baby transformation. The reality star, who welcomed baby Saint in December, has been working hard to get her pre-baby bod back.

    The 35-year-old star signed on with Atkins as their new Brand Ambassador for the Atkins 40: Low Carb Diet Plan.

    "I'm so excited! I'm 30 lbs down today but 40 to go! I'm so focused. I will show u guys my workouts on my app & share my Atkins plan too," she tweeted in January. "I gained 60lbs during my pregnancy but have been an extra 10 lbs up for the last few years so it's time to really get to my goal."

    "This is going to be soooo f--king hard but I can do it and I can't wait to share it all with you! #BeastMode #WhosWithMe?!?!?!" she added.

    In addition to her exercise plan, Mrs. Kanye West is following a protein-and-vegetable-heavy diet, which allows her to snack on low glycemic fruits like berries, and get healthy fats from Greek yogurt, small portions of cheese, and nuts and olive oil.
