    She was the star of ABC's Rebel.

    And Katey Sagal looked like quite the independent woman while getting brunch at Joan's On 3rd in Studio City. 

    The Married...with Children actress, 67, showed off her significant tattoo collection while wearing an all black dress and Birkenstock sandals on the outing.

    Out to brunch: Actress Katey Sagal showed off her rebellious side along with her tattoos while making her way to Joan's On Third in Studio City

    Ready to step out in public, she held a pink face covering in her hand as she walked down the street. 

    The star later emerged from the restaurant holding a coffee and a bag of leftovers.

    Sagal should have a lot of time to go out to brunch with friends since her television show, Rebel, was cancelled in May after just one 10-episode season. 

    Rebel is a legal comedy-drama inspired by the life of Erin Brockovich. It was created by Grey's Anatomy showrunner Krista Vernoff.   

    A star of yesteryear: Sagal is probably best known for her role as Peggy Bundy in Married...with Children which ran for 11 seasons from 1987 to 1997

    Still a rebel: The star has a lot more time to go to brunch since her show, Rebel, was cancelled in May

    Fans have tried to save the show by signing a petition, much in the same way shows like Brooklyn Nine-Nine and Lucifer were saved. More than 92,000 people have signed the petition on change.org to date.  

    The Futurama voice actress also appeared on two episodes of the hit Netflix show Dead to Me last year, where she played the mother of Linda Cardellini's character 

    She will also appear in Tattered Hearts, a made for television horror movie which is set to come out next year. 

    Her most consistent work in recent years has been as Louise Goldufski in the Roseanne spin-off, The Conners.  

    Still a part of the sitcom era: Sagal, pictured here in 2019, has still gotten consistent work in recent years, namely on The Conners, the Roseanne spin-off show

    No anarchy in her persona life: Sagal, seen here on the set of Sons of Anarchy, has had a very calm personal life over the last two decades

    The star's personal life has been about as quiet as her professional life. She married actor Kurt Sutter in 2004, and the pair are still together. The couple has one child together, 14-year-old Esme. 

    This isn't the first time the actress has married though. She had a short, four-year-long union with musician Freddy Beckmeier in the late 1970s and was married to another musician, Fred Lombardo, from 1986 to 1989. 

    She had two children with her third husband, Jack White, who she married in 1993. The couple had two children together Jackson White, 25, and Sarah White, 27. 

    Mother of three: Sagal, seen here on the set of The Conners, has three kids, two with drummer Jack White and one with her current husband, Kurt Sutter
