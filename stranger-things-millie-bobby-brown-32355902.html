    Millie Bobby Brown had all eyes on her as she went braless in a tiny white top that was almost see-through.

    The Stranger Things actress, 20, looked stunning as she held up her phone for a cheeky mirror selfie in a white co-ord comprised of thigh-skimming shorts and a V-neck blouse with one lapel folded down. The top featured tiny cup sleeves and left Millie’s midriff bare, revealing her bellybutton and a swathe of tanned skin.

    Posing up a storm next to a spiral staircase, sandwiched next to a vivid green houseplant, the author added a pair of bright lilac glasses with rounded frames and let her brunette hair flow free behind her. Millie opted for a low-key make-up look for the shoot, made up of a bright pink pout and a hint of silver eyeshadow.
