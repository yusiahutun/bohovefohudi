    Which kind of girl do you want to date or marry? Do you want a girl who is beautiful but doesn't speak much? Or do you want a girl you can have a good conversation with? No girls are alike. You will encounter loads of girls before you find Ms. Right. The different types of girls you will meet each have their distinct personalities.

    Most people categorize girls into two broad categories: good and bad. But do you know that there are so many other adjectives that are used to define the personality of a girl? Girls are defined using terminologies such as cute, beautiful, shy, innocent, adventurous, and others. You need to know a girl's character before you hook up with her.

    List of the different types of girls you'll meet

    Girls are diverse and come in different personalities. But how much do you know about these personalities? Here are all the juicy details you need to know about the types of girls you'll meet.

    1. The adventurous girl

    This is the most common type of girls that guys like. She is the most daring girl who doesn't waste the chance to do something new. For example, she doesn't hesitate to try new foods or visit new destinations. This kind of girl is not your dream girl if you love staying home and relaxing during weekends and holidays. She prefers to take road trips instead of staying at home.

    2. The athletic girl

    If you are looking for unique female personalities, the athletic girl should be at the top of your list. This girl has a deep passion for sports. She can wake up as early as 5 a.m., wear her workout clothes, and go jogging. The athletic girl focuses her attention on keeping her body in shape.

    The people she works out with are her closest confidants. If they don't like you, the chances of winning her over are very minimal. The best thing you can do to win her over is to develop the desire to get your body in shape. She will feel happy when you accompany her to the gym.

    3. Drama queen

    The drama queen will do everything possible to be the centre of attention. It is hard to date such a woman because she is mean and selfish. You should be prepared to be lonely because your friends will avoid you when the drama queen is around. She tends to blow small things out of proportion.

    For example, if you fail to accompany her somewhere, you must have a good excuse for your absence. Otherwise, she will cause so much drama and make you feel guilty. If you date this type of girl, she should always be the first thing on your priority list.

    4. Bossy girl

    The bossy girl is the dominant partner in any relationship. She always has the last say in any argument. You may find yourself in a situation where you have no option but to obey her orders.

    If you feel that she is bossing you over, the best thing you should do is ignore her. Alternatively, you should stand up for yourself and tell her you don't want to be bossed around. This is the type of girl you should avoid dating at all costs.

    5. Motherly girl

    What is a good personality for a girl? This is one of the best girl personalities you will ever meet. The motherly girl is selfless, kind, organized, and caring. She is the type of woman you should date if you want someone to take good care of you.

    The motherly girl will also take good care of your children when they arrive. This type of girl you should date if you are looking for a girl to settle down with.

    6. The nerdy girl

    The nerdy girl is one of the personalities you will encounter in the dating scene. She is the type of girl who is passionate about learning new things. The nerdy girl can be described as funny, outgoing, and adventurous. She has all the good qualities that a man wants in a woman.

    The best part about dating a nerdy girl is that she does not ask for expensive gifts. They are interested in finding true love. Unfortunately, you may face the daunting task of finding such a woman in a social context.

    7. The playgirl

    This is the type of girl that men tend to avoid the most in the dating scene. Playgirls are renowned for their extravagant lifestyle. At the outset, the playgirl may seem perfect because she is beautiful and charming.

    In the dating scene, these types of girls are not loyal to one partner and have many male friends. When you date a playgirl, she will not be settled in the relationship and will always crave a better partner. The chances of getting dumped are very high because she will fall for the next perfect guy.

    8. The fashionista

    The fashionista is the type of girl with a deep passion for fashion. You can quickly identify this girl when you look at her dressing style from head to toe. Her make-up is always on point, and she is likely to choose designer clothes for her outfit. It is not surprising that most men easily fall for her because she appears to be miss perfect.

    9. The nagging girl

    Nagging is one of the terms you can use to describe your type of girl. This type of girl nags her way to make a man do what she wants. If you meet this type of girl, you need to agree with everything she does to avoid conflict.

    She will repeatedly ask for the same thing and will feel upset whenever you fail to meet her demands. The best way to deal with a nagging girl is to avoid her so that you can minimize conflict.

    10. Innocent girl

    An innocent girl is one who does not have much experience when it comes to matters relationships. You are probably the first man she has met, and she has no one to compare you to. If you have a crush on an innocent girl, you need to take things slow. For example, you need to wait a little longer before you try to hug her or take her on a late-night date.

    11. Career girl

    Men are afraid of dating a career girl because they feel that she has it all and they may have nothing to offer. However, you can build a lasting relationship with a career girl if you know the do's and don'ts. These girls value commitment, independence, and privacy. If you meet this type of girl, you should never try to control or push her around.

    12. Party girl

    This is one of the most common types of female personalities. She is the kind of girl who loves partying. The party girl is renowned for her carefree attitude. They are mostly found in a nightclub because they enjoy a crowded dance floor.

    If you have a crush on a party girl, you should be prepared to attend endless parties across town. She is always in the company of male friends. If you are looking for a woman to settle down with, she is not the ideal girl for you.

    13. Daddy's girl

    This type of girl considers her dad to be the most important male figure in her life. Therefore, she tends to compare every man she meets to her dad. If you can't measure up to her dad, the chances of winning her over are very minimal. She will only introduce you to her dad if she is sure about you. Once you win her dad, you already have her heart.

    14. Insecure girl

    If you are stuck on what to look for in a girl, you should assess her insecurity. An insecure girl is one who is extremely jealous and has insecurity issues. Most insecure girls have suffered a heartbreak in the past.

    She is worried about being dumped and will irrationally react when she sees you talking to another girl. You will need to work hard to please her and make her feel secure about the relationship.

    This type of girl can be described as outgoing. The only problem is that she loves talking and doesn't know when to shut her mouth. She'll be more dominant in the relationship, especially if she is dating a shy guy. This kind of woman will help a man get out of his shell.
