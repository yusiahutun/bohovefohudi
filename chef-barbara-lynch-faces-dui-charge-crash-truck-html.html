    Boston celebrity chef Barbara Lynch was charged with alleged drunk driving in Gloucester, Massachusetts early Tuesday morning. 

    The 53-year-old told Boston police that she was driving home from work at one of her restaurants, according to the Gloucester Times.

    The report alleged that she said she had 'a few glasses of wine with dinner' and 'did not know what happened.' She faces an Operating Under the Influence (OUI) charge which is similar to a DUI.
