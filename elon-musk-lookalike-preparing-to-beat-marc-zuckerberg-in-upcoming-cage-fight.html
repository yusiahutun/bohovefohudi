    A doppelganger of the Tesla giant Elon Musk has begun his preparation in case he’s needed to be subbed in during his ‘fight’ against Mark Zuckerburg, as his TikTok’s go viral.

    Recently, Elon challenged Zuckerburg to a celebrity cage fight, and they’ve been teasing each other on Twitter, however, no set date or confirmation has been announced as of yet. Although, the Elon Musk doppelganger isn’t taking any chances.

    Elon Musk doppelganger prepares for ‘fight’ in viral TikToks

    Yi Long Ma, a Chinese TikTok star who has a scarily accurate resemblance to Elon Musk, is preparing for the fight, posting hilarious videos of himself fighting the Facebook founder.

    Of course, it’s not the real Mark, instead, some unfortunate soul wearing a cardboard box on his head with a picture of Zuckerberg. The good sport is willing to get a beating for the millions of views the videos are raking in.

    To add even more humor to the videos, the two can be seen standing in front of a Tesla as they go head to head. The TikTok star can even be seen with an X drawn on his body, after the whole Twitter rebranding drama.

    Fans can’t get over the resemblance

    The videos have been going viral on the platform ever since the fight was ‘announced’ and fans are absolutely loving them. We’ve even turned our post notifications on after our binge session.

    “Is that Elon Musk????” questioned one fan.

    “Bro is in better shape than Elon,” said another.

    Another joked: “This is exactly how I picture Elon when he destroyed Twitter.”

    “I am always surprised when Elon Musk doesn’t actually talk like this,” wrote another fan.

    “@Jake Paul he’s ready for you,” laughed another.

    This isn’t the star’s debut on the social media scene, however. He first went viral a couple of years ago for being Elon’s doppelganger and has since amassed over 1 million followers.

    Zuckerberg says he’s ‘not holding his breath’ for the fight

    Although fans around the world are hoping to watch the Meta boss has recently said he’s ‘not holding his breath’ over the proposed cage fight.

    In a post on the new social media platform Threads, Zuckerberg said he proposed the date of 26 August for the fight. Musk has claimed he’s been training “throughout the day”, as Mark wrote: “I’m ready today… but he hasn’t confirmed.”

    Well, he can always contact Yi Long Ma if he’s getting no response from Elon!
