    Crooked, misaligned, gold, yellow, and gapped. These are just a few words used to describe some of the worst teeth in show biz. In an industry that's fixated on perfection, it's all too surprising when a celeb stays true to their own standard of beauty. But who are celebrities with bad teeth in 2021?

    A good smile is important in many careers. However, the career route of being a celebrity is one where you want your smile looking the best it can. Naturally, this means that many celebrities have had various sessions of dental work done on their less than perfect smiles.

    15 celebrities with bad teeth

    Celebrities have bad teeth, just like any other person would have. Some celebrities would want to fix their bad teeth, but others embrace who they are. Here are 15 celebrities with messed-up teeth:

    1. Anthony Davis

    Anthony Davis is a world-class basketball player, known by many because of his unibrow. His teeth, however, are misaligned and crooked, a situation that does not favour him, especially during light fun moments with friends.

    2. Madonna

    Madonna is an American singer and songwriter, always referred to by many as the “Queen of pop”. Madonna has a gap between her two front teeth. Celebrities like Madonna care much about their public image, and her teeth are one of the aesthetic concerns. She seems to have done some dental activities to narrow the gap between her teeth.

    3. Zac Efron

    Zac Efron is an American singer and actor who began his career in the early 2000s. Efron had a big gap between his teeth that would make him uncomfortable most of the time. He, therefore, visited a dentist to reduce the gap before having many career engagements.

    4. Anna Paquin

    Celebrities worldwide tend to feel uncomfortable with their bad teeth, so many do dental work to repair them. But for Anna Paquin, the story is different as she has left the gap between her teeth to grow. When she was a child, the gap was small, and it has continued to grow.

    5. 50 Cent

    50 Cent has excelled in his career and continues to reach millions of people worldwide. However, his front teeth were larger than the rest of the teeth, which did not stop him from entertaining masses of people. But it was until another rapper, a friend of his, made fun of him, and he decided to correct the problem. He reduced the big teeth to align with the rest of the teeth, but the gap remains.

    6. Kirsten Dunst

    Another celebrity broke the norm of visiting a dentist to fix her teeth, as many celebrities occasionally do. Dunst has crooked teeth, which many people would want her to fix. She grew up with pressure around her and felt she needed to correct the problem but thanks to Sofia Coppola, a film director who encouraged her not to correct her teeth.

    7. Ricky Gervais

    8. Steve Buscemi

    Buscemi has entered into the list of famous people with bad teeth. He has stood firm to reject those who advise him to fix his teeth. The film industry is quite challenging as it seems to favour those who have it all right. But with few celebrities like Buscemi, such a narrative has changed.

    9. Johnny Depp

    Johnny Depp is one of the celebrities with yellow teeth. People took a keen look at Depp's life, especially the teeth, in 2017 when he was involved in a divorce with actress Amber Heard. He has a red tinge on one tooth, and many people were surprised when they first realized dental work on his teeth.

    10. Vanessa Paradis

    Venessa has embraced the gap between her teeth, and she is always proud to smile in public without shying away or closing the gap. In many interviews, she has been asked about her gap; she has consistently said the gap is helpful, and she can even spit water through it.

    11. Michael Strahan

    Michael is a former football player, a journalist, and a television personality known for his charming, gap-toothed smile. He felt a lot of pressure to close the gap when he was a professional footballer, but he decided to embrace the gap and accept who he is.

    Celebrities with messed-up teeth tend to deny themselves, not so with Strahan, who thinks his children cannot recognize him without the gap.

    12. Lindsay Lohan

    Lindsay had discoloured teeth which she fixed and continues to maintain. She was one of the celebrities with the worst teeth in Hollywood, and it made her uncomfortable. Many of her fans did not like the discoloured teeth, and they attributed the problem to her alcohol and drug use lifestyle.

    Duff is one of the celebrities with big teeth, but it doesn't make her beauty fade away. She got porcelain veneers, which are not only brighter white, but also are larger and more symmetrical along their bottom ridges.
