    Since Naked Attraction was first broadcast on screens back in 2016 fans have watched with their jaws on the floor. But it turns out there was one x-rated moment from Chrissie’s episode that didn’t get aired - with the model describing it as “so hot.”

    Speaking exclusively to Daily Star, TV stunner Chrissie gushed over her time on the programme. She said: “It was the best show I’ve filmed in my entire life and I’ve done loads of reality.” As a fan already, she quipped: “Walking in and seeing those pods were a mind f*ck.”

    Filming for the show took “a couple hours” according to Chrissie, who noted how there was “loads of footage.” But apparently, there was a naughty moment that producers failed to air that had Chrissie swooning.

    She explained: “There was like a thing where I was saying how it’s all about the motion in the ocean. And there was one surreal moment which they didn’t show on telly.

    “The men in all my pods, all they had shown so far were their willies, and they were knees bent helicoptering their willies.” Swooning, Chrissie added: “It was like the best thing I had ever seen in my life.”

    A fan of guys with large manhoods, she said: “And because I only asked for thick willies, I just had all these jumbo willies. It was like the porn episode of Dumbo. It was so hot.”
