    Feeling tense? These are the neck massagers fans swear by...

      It’s safe to say almost everyone has suffered with a stiff neck, or upper body tension, at some point in their life - and as I can personally attest, it’s not fun!

      Some may have pulled a muscle, found stress causes tension to build up in the neck and shoulders, have an uncomfortable WFH set up, or slept awkwardly and woken up with a crick in your neck, and a massage is often the key to releasing that stiffness. 

      What are the benefits of an at-home neck massager

      While we can’t always visit a masseuse to work on those knots, having the tools to give yourself a pamper, or expert treatment in the comfort of your own home - and without the hefty price tag - is a welcome solution. 

      Neck massagers can help reduce stress and alleviate aches and pains, and many can also be used for relief in hard-to-reach parts of your body like your lower or upper back area. They're also a time-saving and budget-friendly choice, if you can't go for a professional massage or want to reduce expensive visits to the spa.

      "I first discovered at-home neck massagers in 2020 at the beginning of the pandemic when my neck and shoulders were so stiff and painful from an improvised work from home setup," says HELLO! Online Senior Lifestyle Editor Karen Silas. "I bought an Amazon wraparound Shiatsu style one with multiple speeds and heat settings – and complete with a carry case – and was so surprised at how effective it was. It really works the knots out and makes me feel so much better.

      "With lockdown far behind us, I admit there are quite a few gadgets I impulse bought that are gathering dust. But not my neck massager! I still use it for my neck and shoulders and my lower back, too."

      How we chose our favourite neck massagers

      We have found a whole host of neck massagers to help loosen up those tight areas, from Theragun and Shiatsu heated electric massagers, to manual handheld devices and affordable rollers.  There is something to suit all budgets, but how did we choose the best for our edit?

      • Price: We took cost into consideration, being sure to include devices at various price points including less expensive models that we consider to be a great value for price. 
      • Quality: When we’re not able to test the back massager ourselves, we analyse ratings and reviews from verified shoppers and chose only those with top marks making sure that the pros far outweighed the cons. 
      • Customisable settings:  While a basic back massager might do the trick, we added extra weight to our evaluations for models which feature various speeds and heat settings.
      • Accessories: We love a carry case! Where possible we selected models that are easy to store in their own bag.

      Shop our edit of the best neck massagers
