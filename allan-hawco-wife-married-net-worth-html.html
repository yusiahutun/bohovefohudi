    The creator of Netflix series Frontier, Allan Hawco, is an executive producer, writer, and screenwriter.  He came to the limelight after producing the television series, Republic of Doyle.

    As an actor, Allan has acted in multiple TV shows, including ZOS: Zone of Separation (2009), Republic of Doyle (2010), The Book of Negroes (2015), Caught (2018), Jack Ryan (2019) and more.

    Apart from TV shows, Allan has also been a part of movies like Closing the Ring (2007), Love & Savagery (2009), Hyena Road 2015), Weirdos 2016), and The Child Remains (2017).

    For his contributions to the entertainment industry, Allan was honored with the Canadian Film and Television Hall of Fame's Outstanding Achievement Award (2011). 

    Personal Life: Married To Journalist Wife

    Allan has been married to a woman named Carolyn Stokes for several years now. His wife, Carolyn, is a journalist who works at CBC's Newfoundland & Labrador. Also, she co-hosts another show titled Here & Now.
