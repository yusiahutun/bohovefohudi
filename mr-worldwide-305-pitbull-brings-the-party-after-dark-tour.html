    Mr. Worldwide 305 Pitbull Brings The Party After Dark Tour

    12 Apr 2024, 00:00

    MIAMI (CelebrityAccess) – Today, the renowned artist Pitbull has unveiled his Party After Dark Tour, slated to hit more than 25 cities throughout the United States. Special guest T-Pain and openers SiriusXM Globalization DJs are joining him on this musical journey, with Lil Jon set to appear in select markets. Following a string of successful solo tours and a recent triple headlining spectacle – The Trilogy Tour – alongside Enrique Iglesias and Ricky Martin, Pitbull, also known as Mr. Worldwide, promises another exhilarating tour experience, featuring a vibrant mix of his chart-topping hits and surprises.

    Presented by Live Nation, the 26-city tour commences on August 21 at Jiffy Lube Live in Bristow, VA. It will traverse iconic venues such as Hersheypark Stadium, Forest Hills Stadium, Thunder Ridge Nature Arena, Xfinity Center, and Northwell Health At Jones Beach Theater before concluding on October 5 at Isleta Amphitheater in Albuquerque, NM.

    Pitbull’s recent musical endeavors include the Trackhouse and Trackhouse: Daytona 500 Edition releases, inspired by his co-ownership of the NASCAR Cup Series team Trackhouse Racing since 2021. The albums feature collaborations with renowned artists such as Dolly Parton, Tim McGraw, Nile Rodgers, T-Pain, and Omar Courtz.

    Beyond his musical pursuits, Pitbull continues to champion the transformative work of SLAM! Charter schools – using music as a tool to unite and inspire communities.

    Tickets for the tour go on sale starting Friday (April 26) at 10 am local time on LiveNation.com. Additionally, the tour will offer various VIP packages and experiences, allowing fans to elevate their concert experience with premium tickets, backstage tours, VIP lounges, exclusive gift items, and more.
