    Just off the Avenue des Champs-Élysées in Paris, tucked inside a sunny private courtyard, resides an under-the-radar haven for beauty junkies: the Balmain Hair Couture x Nabil Harlow salon. The nearly 6,000 square-foot space is an ode to the wonders of ivory crown molding, the beauty of tiny chandeliers, and a space where a haircut, blow-out, manicure, or massage are elevated to another plane of Parisian opulence. To put it simply: it's the chicest salon you'll ever visit. And you'll never want to leave.

    It's no wonder the quiet, airy salon has become a hot-spot for supermodels looking to refresh during fashion week. We paid a visit at the start of the fall 2017 haute couture shows to challenge Balmain Hair Couture's Creative Director Nabil Harlow to a major hair transformation. We sent a Parisian woman—with varying demands about her wavy, long hair—to Harlow for a high-fashion, Balmain-worthy hair makeover. At the end of it all, armed with a pair of scissors, a flat iron, and shine-enhancing argan oil, Harlow transformed her hair into an Old Hollywood lob with blunt, short, Cleopatra-esque bangs. It somehow looked longer than when he started; a special kind of haircut magic. Watch the whole process go down, above.
