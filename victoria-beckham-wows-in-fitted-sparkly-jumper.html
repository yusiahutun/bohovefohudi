      Victoria's former hairdresser Jason Collier recently spoke to HELLO! about some of her nifty styling secrets, revealing that she has an incredible face contouring trick. 

      "It's what we call a kind of 'hair contouring'," he said. "What I would do to highlight the cheekbones, is anything from the back of the ear we always blow-dry a little bit flatter and at an angle - so it slims down the face but then kind of highlights the cheeks a little bit."
