    Chrissie Williams - Tina Hobley

    Tina, who plays Chrissie Williams in Holby, certainly isn’t afraid to strip herself bare for keen soap fans. Though she has posed completely naked in the past, Tina does "wonder why" she did the pics.

    She told The Sun as she prepared to compete on skiing contest programme The Jump in 2016: "I do look back and wonder why I did them. It was because someone, somewhere, put me in front of a camera and told me to do those pictures.

    "I’m quite a good girl so I do what I’m told to do," she confessed. Regardless, Tina regularly sets pulses racing, and she’s definitely a water baby as she recently braved a chilly-looking lake with a few pals for her birthday celebrations.

    She sported a V-necked skintight swimming costume in black. Tina treated her Instagram followers to a glimpse of her incredible thighs as she popped out of the water to raise her arms high in the air.

    She was rocking a vintage-style floral pink swimming cap as she rode the waves for some open water swimming, and later posed alongside pals wrapped in just a towel. "Then this happened... and why not!" Tina wrote alongside the photoset.

    Fans including singer-songwriter Natalie Imbruglia rushed to share their love. The pop star wrote: "Happy Birthday!" with a love-heart emoji.

    Donna Jackson - Jaye Jacobs

    Holby’s very own Donna Jackson, played by Jaye Jacobs, also made a name for herself in BBC school drama Waterloo Road from 2011 until 2013, as deputy headteacher Sian Diamond. She’s also appeared in everything from Strictly Come Dancing - though she was eliminated in the second week - to EastEnders spin-off Perfectly Frank.

    The stunning senior nurse keeps her personal life rather private, but she isn't afraid to let her hair down, and has been snapped having plenty of fun with a pal on a night out. Sporting a jaw-dropping orange, off-the-shoulder dress, Jaye flaunted her gorgeous curves in the clingy ruched number as she had a giggle along with a pal.

Her brunette tresses were teased into gentle waves that fell just past her shoulders, and she opted for a glam makeup look for the chic shot.

    Ange Godard - Dawn Steele

    Scottish actress Dawn Steele is best known as Ange Godard in Holby City. But she’s also had prominent roles in BBC drama Monarch of the Glen, ITV’s Wild at Heart and BBC soap River City.

    Like Tina, Dawn loves spending time by the water, and recently offered an intimate snap of herself posing by the sea in a stunning swimwear set. Bending down to capture the best selfie angle, Dawn’s cleavage was on full display as the wind whipped her brunette tresses around her.

    The gorgeous blue sky was no match for Dawn, as she had fans absolutely gobsmacked by the stunning picture, as her swimming costume hugged her toned waist and revealed her tanned thighs. Pairing the look with some dark sunglasses, Dawn later looked introspective as she stared wistfully out onto the horizon, standing by the shore.

    The mum-of-one posed with a hand on her hip as she bared her impressive body for the camera. At the time, the BBC released a statement which read: "We are incredibly proud of Holby City but it's with great sadness that we are announcing that after 23 years, the show will end on screen in March of next year.
