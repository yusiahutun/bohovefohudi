    Happy Birthday to popstar Lady Gaga, YouTuber Zoella and actor Vince Vaughn! To celebrate their big days, we take a look at the lives of these famous birthday girls and boys…

    Lady Gaga turns 32

    Born This Way hitmaker Lady Gaga celebrates her 32nd birthday today! It has been a year of ups and downs for the popstar, who reportedly got engaged to her talent manager boyfriend Christian Carino last summer. She was recently seen making a coffee run with Christian, displaying a huge ring on her engagement finger as she clasped her coffee cup. However, it has also been a difficult 12 months for Gaga, as she was forced to cancel the last leg of her Joanne World Tour due to suffering from a condition named fibromyalgia, which causes chronic pain. Recently, the singing sensation was out and about in California supporting the March For Our Lives protests, which are demanding stricter gun control in America.

    RELATED: Lady Gaga opens up about her experiences with fibromyalgia

    Zoella turns 28

    Happy Birthday to YouTube sensation Zoella! The online star, who has over 12 million YouTube subscribers as well as her own beauty and lifestyle ranges, is jetting off to celebrate her birthday in New York with her nearest and dearest, including her boyfriend and fellow YouTuber Alfie Deyes, his sister Poppy, Zoe’s best friend Mark Ferris and her brother Joe. Zoe was also treated to a surprise pre-birthday celebration at her office. The blogger and entrepreneur shared a snap of her party, telling her 11 million Instagram followers: “I’m so lucky that the people I work with are so amazing and kind hearted”. She was presented with a stunning three-tiered birthday cake, featuring tropical prints and a rose gold pineapple. Zoe also had an “early fake birthday” whilst shooting photos for her upcoming book, Cordially Invited, which will be her first non-fiction literary venture.

    Vince Vaughn turns 48

    Happy Birthday to Hollywood actor Vince Vaughn! The Wedding Crashers star was recently seen spending some quality time with his dad Vernon, as the pair played blackjack in Las Vegas. This has been a year of change for the renowned comedy actor. In his latest film, Brawl In Cell Block 99, released last year, he plays a prisoner - a character far removed from his usual comedic roles.
