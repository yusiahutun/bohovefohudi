    Steve's relationship with Sherrie Swafford was the most outspoken. Their love was so strong that they were seen as the Romeo and Juliet of the 1980s. Steve Perry loved Sherrie so much that he featured her in the song Oh Sherrie, one of the singer’s hit songs in his first solo album, Street Talk, released in 1984.

    From how things turned out, people concluded that maybe Sherrie was the one and Steve would marry her one day. But unfortunately, things did not turn out as many people envisioned. So, what happened to Steve Perry and Sherrie Swafford?

    Steve was busy with music and had many tours, resulting in intense pressure on their relationship. Therefore, the romance dwindled, and they broke up. After their separation, Swafford disappeared from the limelight and was not heard of until blog author Mac Tyler Nobleman caught up with her in 2013.
