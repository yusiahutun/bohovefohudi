    During last night’s episode of his TBS show, Conan O’Brien revealed two of the guests slated to appear on his next remote special Conan Without Borders: Made In Mexico next month. In addition to Rogue One star Diego Luna, O’Brien will chat with former Mexican President Vicente Fox during the special, and as O’Brien points out, Fox is more recently known for being one of the most unapologetic voices when it comes to Donald Trump claiming Mexico will pay for the wall:

    TRUMP, when will you understand that I am not paying for that fucken wall. Be clear with US tax payers. They will pay for it.

    — Vicente Fox Quesada (@VicenteFoxQue) January 6, 2017

    Do not be afraid of Trump, he’s a bully and a bluff, bullies end when they’re called out. America, stand out before it’s too late!!

    — Vicente Fox Quesada (@VicenteFoxQue) January 11, 2017

    Conan Without Borders: Made In Mexico airs on Wednesday, March 1st at 10:00pm.
