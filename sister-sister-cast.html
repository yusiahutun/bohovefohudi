    While most of us can’t say we’ve ever come across a long-lost twin sibling in a shopping mall, that’s not to say it doesn’t make for great television! Sister, Sister, the iconic 90s sitcom cast starring real-life twin sisters Tia and Tamera Mowry, tells the story of two girls who experience a once-in-a-lifetime reunion while shopping with their adoptive parents.

    The duo, who were separated at birth, reunite as teens in this chance encounter, and in an effort to keep the duo from further separation, their parents join forces, with Lisa and Tia moving in with Ray and Tamera, allowing the girls to experience their teens as sisters. This laugh-out-loud comedy launched the careers of Tia and Tamera Mowry, and this sitcom remains one of our favorites to this day. Here, take a look at the Sister, Sister cast today!

    Sister, Sister cast, then and now

    Tia Mowry as Tia Landry

    Tia Landry, the more academically-minded sister of the duo, was played by Tia Mowry. After the show came to its conclusion in 1999, however, Mowry did not slow down. Both she and her sister continued their education at Pepperdine University, while also continuing to act. After Sister, Sister, some of Tia’s most notable roles were in Seventeen Again (2000), The Hot Chick (2002), Twitches (2005) and Twitches Too (2007), all in which she starred alongside her sister Tamera Mowry. She later went on to have starring television roles in The Game and Instant Mom.

    In recent years, she’s found success doing numerous television movies, those including My Christmas Inn (2018), A Gingerbread Romance (2018) on Hallmark, A Very Vintage Christmas (2019) and more. Some of her most recent roles were in Yes, Chef! Christmas (2023) and the Netflix series, Family Reunion.

    Outside of acting, she’s shown her multitude of talents in other areas: she hosted the successful cooking series Tia Mowry at Home, and has also authored several books like Oh, Baby: Pregnancy Tales and Advice from One Hot Mama to Another, The Quick Fix Kitchen and the Twintuition book series, which she co-wrote with her sister.

    Tamera Mowry-Housley as Tamera Campbell

    After Sister, Sister, Tamera Mowry, who played the fun-loving Tamera Campbell in the cast, stood alongside her sister for many projects in the wake of their sitcom success. Seventeen Again (2000), The Hot Chick (2002), Twitches (2005) and Twitches Too (2007) put her alongside Tia. She had a starring role from 2004 to 2006 in the series Strong Medicine.

    One of her most significant roles post-Sister, Sister was as a co-host on the daytime talk show The Real. You can also find Tamera in films across the Hallmark Channel, such as Dream Moms (2023), Girlfriendship (2022), A Christmas Miracle (2019), The Santa Stakeout (2021) and more.

    In 2022, her memoir, You Should Sit Down for This: A Memoir about Life, Wine, and Cookies was published. She is also the co-author of the Twintuition book series with her sister Tia.

    Jackée Harry as Lisa Landry

    Aside from being iconic in her hilarious role of Lisa Landry, Jackée Harry made history as the first African American woman to win an Emmy for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series for her work on the series 227.

    After Sister, Sister however, she’s only continued to shine. She went on to have recurring roles on shows like Everybody Hates Chris and Let’s Stay Together. She also starred in the series The First Family and The Paynes. And most recently, she’s had a recurring role in the iconic soap, Days of Our Lives.

    Tim Reid as Ray Campbell

    Prior to Sister, Sister, Tim Reid was known for his roles in WKRP in Cincinatti, Simon & Simon and Frank’s Place, but his role as Ray Campbell is perhaps what we love him most for. After the series came to an end in the late 90s, Reid acted in television movies such as Alley Cats Strike and You Wish! before taking on a role in That ’70s Show. Over the years, Reid has continued to make appearances in both television and film on series such as Me, Myself and I, Greenleaf, Grey’s Anatomy, Swagger and movies like A New Orleans Noel (2022), Stranger Next Door (2022) and A Welcome Home Christmas (2020).

    Marques Houston as Roger Evans in the Sister, Sister cast

    While making a name for himself as Tia and Tamera’s annoying next door neighbor on Sister, Sister, Marques Houston was also enjoying a successful music career as a member of the group Immature. Pursuing a solo career in 2003, Marques Houston has continued to enjoy success as a musician into present day. As far as his work on the screen after his role of Roger, he could be seen in movies like You Got Served (2003), Fat Albert (2004) and Battlefield America (2012).

    RonReaco Lee as Tyreke Scott in the Sister, Sister cast

    RonReaco Lee played Tia’s boyfriend Tyreke, and after the show also continued to act. Lee had guest roles on shows like Moesha, ER, Girlfriends, All About the Andersons, Committed, The Shield, and later on had more significant parts in Let’s Stay Together, Survivor’s Remorse and First Wives Club. His most recent television role has been in The Black Hamptons.

    Deon Richmond as Jordan Bennett in the Sister, Sister cast

    Deon Richmond, who played Tamera’s college boyfriend Jordan, continued acting throughout the 2000s and into present day. Some films you could see him in include Scream 3 (2000), Not Another Teen Movie (2001), National Lampoon’s Van Wilder (2002) and Hatchet (2006). His most recent roles were in Bad Connection (2023), Cloudy with a Chance of Christmas (2022), and What Matters (2018).
