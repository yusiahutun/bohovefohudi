    25 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Diane Kruger is currently mourning the loss of her beloved stepfather.

    The 40-year-old actress, who is usually reserved about her personal life, shared her grief on Instagram early on Wednesday morning after the death of her mother's longtime partner, Wolfgang Bieneck. 

    The German beauty shared a sweet throwback photo of her mother, Maria-Theresia Heidkrueger and Wolfgang, along with the message, 'Thank you for making my mom so happy and bringing peace to our family. R.I.P.'

    Sad news: Diane Kruger shared this sweet photo of her mother, Maria-Theresia Heidkrueger and her longtime partner Wolfgang Bieneck, after Bieneck's death on Wednesday

    In mourning: The 40-year-old actress, who is usually reserved about her personal life, shared her grief on social media after her stepfather's passing

    The black and white image shows a close-up shot of her mother leaning into her stepfather's shoulder affectionately as the pair smile brightly. 

    Diane's mother and biological father, Hans-Heinrich Heidkrüger, divorced when she was just a young teenager.

    The private star hasn't spoken out much about her family, but back in 2011 she talked to Independent about her dad's battle with alcoholism. 

    'Everyone has issues. But because of my Dad's illness, my Mum had to go through a lot. She was the one that had to go to work and raise us kids alone,' she said. 

    Happier times: Diane's mother and biological father, Hans-Heinrich Heidkrüger, divorced when she was a teen, but she appeared close to her stepfather as he joined the star and her mother for the 2010 Goldene Kamera Awards in Germany

    Family photo: Diane and her mother Maria-Theresia (Center Left) and her partner Wolfgang (2nd from Right) along with Joshua Jackson (R) and her brother Stefan Heidkrueger (2nd from Left) and his girlfriend (L) attended an event in Germany in 2014

    In 2013 Diane gave a little more insight into her family as Marie Claire U.K. quoted the star as saying 'I'm sure I punished many boys for my lack of a father figure...' 

    However, Diane appeared to be close to her stepfather as he and her mother would sometimes attend a glitzy celebrity event with their famous daughter.

    Back in 2010 they accompanied the star to the Goldene Kamera Awards in Berlin, Germany. 

    The whole family, including Diane's younger brother Stefan, attended another event in Germany together back in 2014 along with Diane's then-boyfriend Joshua Jackson.  

    This has been a rough few months for the star as Diane and Joshua ended their 10-year romance in July.
