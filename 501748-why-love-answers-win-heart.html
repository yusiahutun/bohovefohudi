You're not alone. Fortunately, there are tons of "Why do you love me answers for him?" That's from cute and funny replies to thoughtful answers.

    There are so many reasons that can make your boyfriend want to know, "Why do you love him?" It can be because he feels insecure about the relationship or is deeply uncertain about himself. Or he might find it hard to love himself at the moment or in general. So he turns to the person he values, loves, and trusts for validation that he is indeed lovable and worthy of love.

    What to say when your boyfriend asks why you love him?

    There's no perfect answer to the question, "why do you love me?" But the best reply to "why do you love me?" is that which is personal, romantic, and funny.
