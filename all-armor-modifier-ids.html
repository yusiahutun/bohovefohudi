    How to Attach Weapon Modifiers and Attachments with Cheat Codes

    There are 32 armor modifiers in Starfield, which are all split between three different modifier slots - Slot 1, Slot 2, and Slot 3.

    With upwards of three armor modifiers allowed on each spacesuit, helmet, or boost pack, you can attach one modifier from each of the three modifier slots. But, should you attempt to attach two or more modifiers that fit the same slot category, it will be simply replaced with the newest modifier.

    To attach armor modifiers and attachments in Starfield, you'll want to use the following cheat code:

    • (Ref ID Code).amod (OMOD ID)

    To find your armor's reference ID, drop the armor piece on the ground, open the console command, and click on the weapon. Your "ARMO (ID Code)" will now be displayed.
