    Dr Dee Alaska Vet biography

    Dr Dee Thornell was born on 29th July 1958 in Ohio, USA. Her full name is Deanna Thornell. She is the daughter of Leonard Thornell and Geraldine Thornell. Leonard worked as a Marine during the Second World War.

    After retiring from marine, her father mastered the art of tinkering and also worked as a mechanist. Her mother, on the other hand, was a housewife and farmer. Dee is the youngest kid among four children. She has three elder siblings known as Jerrie, Pam and Richard Thornell.

    Her family's stay in Ohio City was short-lived as they left the city while she was in second grade. The choice to leave the city was not voluntary but rather due to a tragedy that befell her family. Richard, her elder brother, passed away when he was 20 years old while fighting for his country in Vietnam.
