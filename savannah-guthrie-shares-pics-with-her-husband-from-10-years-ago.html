    We love a good power couple! Not only is Savannah Guthrie a superstar Today show cohost and an admirable mom-of-two, but she’s also an amazing wife to her husband, Michael Feldlman. In honor of the couple being together for almost a decade, Savannah took to Instagram earlier today to share some sweet pics of the two over the years.

    “But we dated a looooong time. Ten years ago this week! #tenyearchallenge,” the 47-year-old captioned the collage of photos. In the first pic, Savannah and her hubby are seen smiling while out to dinner. In the second, the brunette beauty snapped a cute selfie as Michael planted a sweet kiss on her cheek as a smile beamed from her face. Take a look at the adorable pics below and be sure to scroll right!

    Savannah and Michael have created an amazing life together: the couple tied the knot in 2014, they are each successful in their own careers and together share their two beautiful children, Vale, 4, and Charley, 2. However, the two almost never even got married!

    The Emmy award-winning journalist once revealed that her now-hubby’s proposal back in the day wasn’t necessarily one for the books. During a Today segment this past October, the mom-of-two revealed that she actually broke up with Michael the day he proposed.

    “We did break up on the day that we got engaged,” she said of their 2013 engagement. Without giving away too many intimate details, the TV personality went on to explain that she “broke up with him in the morning and they got engaged in the afternoon” during a trip to Turks and Caicos. “That’s a quick comeback,” her cohost, Al Roker, joked. “It really was,” Savannah quipped back.

    At the time of their engagement, Mike called into Today and admitted that he was nervous to ask for Savannah’s hand in marriage. “I was very, very, very happy when she said yes,” he gushed at time. “I’m the luckiest guy in the world.” We are so happy for them!
