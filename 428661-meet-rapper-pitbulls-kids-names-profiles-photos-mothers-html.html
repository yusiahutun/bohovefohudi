This article shares interesting facts the public knows about Pitbull's kids and their mothers.

    Armando Christian Pérez (alias Pitbull) needs no introduction in the global rap music arena. He is globally known for making awesome reggaetón, Latin hip-hop, and crunk music. The 42-year-old (as of 2023) was born on January 15, 1981, in Miami, Florida, USA. His career began in the early 2000s with his debut album, M.I.A.M.I. The Cuban-American rapper has released over twelve studio albums and over 300 singles, and by 2020, Pitbull had sold over 25 million studio albums and over 100 million singles worldwide.

    Pitbull is the father of six, but only two of his children, Destiny Pérez and Bryce Pérez, are known to the public. The rapper had these two with ex-girlfriend Barbara Alba, whom he dated for almost a decade.
