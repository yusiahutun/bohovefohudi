    Hottest and Coldest Temperatures Ever in Pittsburgh on May 13

    23 May 2024, 00:00

    On May 13th in Pittsburgh, the temperature has seen extremes over the years. The hottest temperature on this day occurred in 1991, reaching a scorching 90.0°F, while the coldest temperature was recorded in 1996, plummeting to a frigid 30.9°F. However, the average temperature for this date stands at a pleasant 58.51°F, with an average high of 65.47°F and an average low of 51.56°F. In an interesting news item from these years, in 1996, the Pittsburgh Pirates baseball team won the National League East division title, marking their first postseason appearance in 11 years.
