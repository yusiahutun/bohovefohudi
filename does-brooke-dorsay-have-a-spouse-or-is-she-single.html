    Brooke D’Orsay, as you know, is a Canadian actress, best known for voicing the character of Caitlin Cooke in the Teletoon animated series 6teen (2004–2010) and Brooke Mayo in the 2005 movie King’s Ransom. In 2022, lately, she returned to her audience one complete year later with a new Hallmark movie, Wedding of a Lifetime. And naturally, everyone wanted to know, what her relationship status has been like these days or even in the past.

    Here’s what we know.

    Does Brooke D’Orsay Have A Spouse?

    No! Brooke D’Orsay did not have a spouse as of 2022. She was previously engaged to fellow American actor Greg Coolidge and though it looked like a very serious one, Brooke did not end up getting married to him.

    After keeping the relationship private for some time, Brooke finally blurted about her dating scenario with Greg, in early August 2012. To a media outlet, she exchanged that Greg had been her rock supporter since the early days of her acting career. Of course, she also had gushed about Greg’s accomplishments. Also a writer and filmmaker, Greg is best known to have acted in and also been a director in Employee of the Month, and for roles in Ride Along, The Troop, The Turkey Bowl, and Wayne.

    Reportedly, Brooke and Greg even were planning to tie the knot in the early summer of 2013. But, that year we saw no news of their likely already planned wedding ceremony. Then, the couple only surfaced on the news in 2016 when Brooke rented their LA-based joint property.

    So, no one knew if they even got married or not.

    Ever since Brooke has close to never talked about her personal life with Greg or anyone else. But, Greg certainly did tweet about his “amazing lady” Jill Scheid one too many times mostly between 2014 and 2016. This silent Jill also while confessing to love him wrote things like “Happy birthday to the most amazing guy I know ❤️”.

    This frequent spotting of the new pair made fans raise questions about Greg’s relationship with Brooke. The rumors of their split got further hyped when the prior-said LA house, owned by both, was put on sale.

    Brooke D’Orsay Relationship Status

    Brooke D’Orsay appeared single as of 2022. This is because she kept her personal life strictly private. She not even hinted at anything on her social media. She also has not involved herself with any public romance rumors since Greg.

    How Old Is Brooke D’Orsay?

    Born on 17 February 1982, Brooke D’Orsay reached the age of 40 in 2022.

    Brooke D’Orsay Height

    Blonde and natural beauty, Brooke D’Orsay stands 5′ 5″ (1.65 meters) in height.

    How Much Is Brooke D’Orsay’s Net Worth?

    Brooke D’Orsay made it to the “richest celebrities” list of Celebrity Net Worth back in 2016. Around that time, the outlet expected her to have above $3 million net worth.

    Back in the day, she owned a single-story home (built in 1954) with Greg. They later sold this midcentury modern-style property, with walls of glass, three fireplaces, and commanding views of the surrounding hillsides. The public records show that Greg bought the house back in 2006 for $2.009 million.

    As for Brooke only, she has been earning her living likely long before she got her first major acting role in 2001, in the movie Why Can’t I Be a Movie Star?

    Following this, Brooke followed with several roles in television: Ellen in Doc, Justine in Soul Food, and Felicity Fury in four episodes of Ace Lightning. She also appeared on The Big Bang Theory as Christy in the episode “The Dumpling Paradox” and in How I Met Your Mother as Margaret in the episode “The Stinsons”. She also went on to notably play Kate, Walden Schmidt’s girlfriend, in a recurring role on Two and a Half Men from 2012 through 2014.

    Who Are Brooke D’Orsay’s Parents?

    Brooke D’Orsay’s parents are Jackie D’Orsay (mom) and William D’Orsay (dad). It is also known that Brooke’s dad once/or even now work at the same university. Her father lectured while her mother worked as a lab assistant in the Chemistry department.

    As for siblings, Brooke has a brother named John D’Orsay.

    Brooke’s family traces their lineage to the French ethnoreligious protestant group called Huguenots who settled in Canada back in the 17th century.
