    Veronica Castro Biography (Age)

    Veronica Judith Saenz Castro Alba, born on October 19, 1952, has been working as an actress since 1964. She was born in Mexico City, D.F. Veronica has long been active both as a singer and as an actress. In 1973 she recorded her first album, Veronica Castro, and subsequently released three hit singles from her next album, Sensaciones. In 1974 she recorded another hit single, “Aprendi A Llorar”. This was just the beginning of a musical career that produced many beautiful songs.

    As an actress, she appeared in many films and television works. She became famous with the telenovela Los Rico Tambien lloran, in which she played a poor orphan. When the sitcom with a single camera attracted a lot of attention from viewers around the world, Veronica’s popularity touched the sky. Two years later, she starred in El derecho de nacer, another popular telenovela. She also began recording her music in various languages as her fame skyrocketed.

    Veronica Castro performed a Latin version of “We Are The World” in 1986. She subsequently released one of her most ambitious albums to date, Simplemente Todo. Since most of her songs were accompanied by videos, which was rather unusual for songs at that time, she became one of the pioneers of the MTV era in Latin America. In the meantime, she experienced a career boost in 1987 when she played the main character in Rosa Salvage. The title song of this telenovela, which was also named after the film, became one of her greatest hits. In the 1990s, Castro recorded many singles and participated in various television shows. To this day she still hosts a large number of shows. Most recently she was cast as Virginia de la Mora in Netflix’s comedy-drama series “The House of Flowers”. The web television series created by Manolo Caro sometimes premiered in August 2018.

Well, Veronica Castro has indeed amassed considerable wealth from her career. In January 2018, her net worth was estimated at around $30 million.
