    Hailey Bieber is in the running to become queen of the naked dress.

    The model's latest Instagram post includes portrait shots of her latest look, a new iteration of the celeb-loved look. In the pics, Bieber poses in a brown strapless gown, comprised of an opaque bandeau panel covering the chest and a sheer, skintight bodice with a hip-high slit, with her black underwear showing through.

    The Rhode founder accessorized the semi-sheer piece with dainty jewelry, with the glimmer of an earring showing through her lightly waved hair. She also included a selfie in the carousel, giving a close-up of her glowing complexion and two-toned nude lip.

    Earlier this year, Bieber stepped out in a sophisticated, all-black spin on the trend for the launch of Tiffany & Co.'s Lock Collection in Los Angeles. She layered Saint Laurent's transparent wool maxi dress with long sleeves and a chimney collar over a triangle bralette and high-waisted underwear, finishing the look with crisscrossing strappy sandals, also from Saint Laurent. Her accessories included a gold collar necklace and several sparkling bangles.

    Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

    Bieber previously opened up about developing her personal sense of style in an interview for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story, revealing that she looks to the late Princess Diana for fashion inspiration.

    "I was really inspired by the fact that she was the most-looked-at woman in the world at that time, of all time, and she did what she wanted with her style," she explained. "She really expressed herself through her style despite being in the position she was in."
