    Zoe Saldana Wears Five Different Glamorous Outfits in New York City

    07 Feb 2024, 00:00

    On a typical day, it is likely I will go through dozens of combinations of clothes (making a disaster of my room in the process) before finally settling on a fabulous outfit (usually, annoyingly, the first look I tried on). My morning fashion show never leaves the confines of my bedroom. But if you’re Zoe Saldana, it does. The actor made the New York City streets her runway on Thursday when she stepped out in five different looks.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    The Avatar star began her full day of engagements in a business casual look that featured a gorgeous pop of color. Her ensemble consisted of a crisp white camisole with lace accents along the low-cut neckline, tucked into a pair of wide-leg jeans in a medium wash. She added black accents in the form of a silky oversize blazer and glossy pointed-toe pumps. Her ensemble served as the perfect canvas for her eye-catching accessory, in this season’s It girl hue: a cherry-red leather purse with gold hardware. Saldana kept her jewelry simple with a couple of gold rings and dainty hoop earrings, wore natural makeup, and swept her hair back into a neat half-up, half-down ’do.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    The Guardians of the Galaxy actor was later seen in TriBeCa with her husband, producer and director Marco Perego. This time, she kept things laid back, with light-wash baggy jeans, the same white camisole, a sleek long gray coat, and white sneakers. She accessorized with a silver charm necklace, gold hoops, and a black crossbody bag, and pulled her locks into a slicked-back bun. Perego looked cool beside her in his own baggy jeans (his a medium wash), black T-shirt, rustic brown leather jacket, and two-tone brown hiking boots.

    Jason Howard/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Later, Saldana went full business-chic in a dark gray button-up midi dress, an icy-white cropped jacket, black pointed-toe slingback heels, and the same shiny red purse. Perego swapped his blue jeans and hiking boots for black slacks and dressy black boots. The couple looked so in love as they posed for pictures, each wearing a sweet smile.

    Raymond Hall//Getty Images

    For her next trick, Saldana embodied an office siren, cutting a sexy figure in an inky- black figure-hugging leather pencil skirt that featured a scooped bottom hem with frilly ruffle details, and a flouncy midnight-black blouse that featured long sleeves, a plunging neckline, and ruffle details draped across the front. She finished off the sultry evening outfit with pointed-toe heels, a dainty gold anklet, a stringy gold necklace, and a small slate-blue bucket purse.

    NBC//Getty Images

    The movie star closed out her busy day on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, where she looked dreamy in a sand-brown jumpsuit. The graceful piece featured a one-shoulder design, three-quarter sleeves, a clingy construction along her torso and hips, and flowy pants. Saldana wore the creation with sheer black tights, matching heels, a diamond-adorned gold bracelet, matching necklace, and diamond-encrusted hoop earrings (jewelry by Vrai).
