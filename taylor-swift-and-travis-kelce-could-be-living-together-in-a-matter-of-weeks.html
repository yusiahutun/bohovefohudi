    All eyes are still shining on Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s relationship, and they could be living together in just a matter of weeks, according to moving experts.

    Rumors claimed last year, when the music star had a break from her Eras Tour, she had temporarily moved in with her athlete boyfriend, although this wasn’t permanent, reportedly. When Travis Kelce and the Chiefs stormed the Super Bowl, a lot of fans were hoping he proposed to the star. But to their disappointment, that didn’t happen. We didn’t see Trav get down on one knee for Taylor Swift, but we did hear his adorable first three words he said to her when he saw his girlfriend on the field. Now, experts are weighing in on when they’ll take the next step. Some believe Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce will be living together in just a few short weeks.

    The hottest couple in Hollywood ‘aren’t slowing down’

    Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce could be set to elevate their relationship by officially living in together this March, as they take advantage of the lull between major events.  

    Tes, a moving expert at resource site moveBuddha, believes fans of the NFL tight end and the Grammy-winning star could see the pair move into Trav’s mansion in Kansas, or Taylor’s Nashville estate in a matter of weeks.

    She says: “Speculation was rife that Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce had started to share a home together for the long-term in late November, as the singer moved in with her NFL boyfriend for a brief period in the two-month break from The Eras Tour.

    “However, this cohabitation was temporary, meaning the major relationship milestone is yet to be reached. 

    “We expect to see the duo officially move in together before the end of March, as they can take a break from their intense career schedules and shift their attention to more personal priorities.

    “The relationship is clearly going well, and both parties’ families were pictured together at the Super Bowl, so there’s no reason to believe that things will slow down.”

    Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce living together is the ‘best way to celebrate’

    The duo have seen a great year individually, with the Chiefs winning the Super Bowl after a nail-biting season, and Taylor’s Eras Tour already raking in more than $1 billion.

    Tes doesn’t believe the celebrity couple will buy a new property together, but instead, we may see Taylor Swift or Travis Kelce living together in one of their own homes. They would keep their own real estate properties as well, according to the expert.

    Tes explains: “Taylor has been spotted at both of the homes Travis owns in Kansas, but the newer purchase is in a gated community that offers the security that the high-profile couple needs. Since it’s a newer purchase, it’s highly likely that it was made with the couple’s future in mind – therefore, it may be a given that this will be the home they both move into next month. 

    “However, the duo may choose one of Taylor’s eight multi-million properties to share. If so, it’ll probably be her 5,600 sq. ft. Greek Revival Nashville home, Northumberland Estate. Tennessee is closer to Kansas than her other properties (in New York and Rhode Island), which would be key for Travis to stay with the Kansas City Chiefs.”

    It’s the ‘best’ time because of Taylor’s busy schedule

    Tes continues Taylor will be too busy juggling “promo for her new album, awards season, and packed tour schedule” to think about buying up a new home with Travis, brother to athlete Jason Kelce.

    Taylor’s new album, The Tortured Poets Department, would have come out by April, and when she’s wrapping up her UK leg of the Eras Tour in the summer, Trav will be looking to the future of the new NFL season in September.

    Therefore, spring seems the best option, compared to early or late summer, Tes explains.

    Tes adds: “Swift can’t realistically jet across to Kansas from the UK whenever she gets a break between shows without risking exhaustion, so it makes sense that they’d want something big to look forward to, like buying a home together.” 

    It comes after a celebrity psychic told CelebrityTidbit he sees an engagement on the cards in a matter of months. So they better hurry, if Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce want to be living together for some time before the 34-year-old pops the big question.
