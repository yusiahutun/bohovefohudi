    It was expected that the daughter of renowned Chinese actor Andrew Tinpo Lee would enter the acting profession. Peyton Elizabeth Lee, who has followed in her father’s footsteps, is a famous American teenage actress. According to the talented entertainer, her father is a great inspiration to her, and she took up the acting profession only to find a purpose in life like him.

    She made her acting debut at the tender age of 12 when she was cast as spelling competitor Violet in the thriller television series “Scandal”. Since her debut, the child star has become very popular and can be found on various social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

    Peyton Elizabeth Lee Bio (Age)

    The child actress Peyton Elizabeth Lee was born on May 22, 2004, in New York City as the daughter of the American mother Jennifer Dormer Lee, who currently works as a psychologist. Her father is of course Andrew Tinpo Lee, the famous Chinese actor. Peyton grew up with her parents together with an older sister named Ella Margaret Lee and a younger brother Julian Andrew Lee.

    The child star of mixed ethnicity is, as at the time of this report, the youngest acting student ever to be accepted at Playhouse West Acting School. And in 2014, when Peyton Elizabeth Lee turned 10 years old, she completed acting training in two studios in Los Angeles, California.

    Peyton has been in the entertainment industry since 2015, and she made her acting debut the same year when she appeared in the Kerry Washington thriller television series called Scandal, in which she was cast as Violet, a spelling bee. The drama television series entitled Shameless, which aired in 2016, but the child star in the spotlight as she appeared in three different episodes in the role of a soldier. She is currently starring in Andi Mack, a comedy television series that premiered on the Disney Channel on April 7, 2017.

    Her Parents and Family Life

    Again, Elisabeth is the daughter of Andrew Tinpo Lee, who happens to be a famous Chinese actor, and according to the child star, her father is the reason why she is interested in this profession. Her mother is Jennifer Dormer Lee, is American, Jennifer works as a psychologist.

    Elizabeth’s family life revolves around her parents and siblings. As mentioned above, she is a younger sister of Ella Margaret Lee and has a younger brother named Julian Andrew Lee. Peyton and her siblings are of mixed descent. They received their Chinese heritage from their father and are Americans thanks to their mother.
