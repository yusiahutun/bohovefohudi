    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Katherine Schwarzenegger says “I Do”… at least when it comes to modeling wedding dresses.

    The fashion blogger, New York Times Best selling author, and daughter of Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Shriver looks gorgeous in a new promo shoot for the 2017 Atelier Pronovias Preview Collection.

    Shot at a private villa in Pasadena, California, the 26-year-old stunner is a natural in front of the camera for her first major modeling gig.

    “The collection is beautifully made and dresses are delicate and very comfortable,” she says in a statement. “I could easily see these being worn on a red carpet, to a gala, or a black tie event. In three words, I would sum up the new collection as chic, feminine and sexy.”

    “This is the first time I have worked with Pronovias and I truly enjoyed the experience,” she adds.

    Schwarzenegger previously opened up to Hello! about the shoot, explaining that her goal was to promote body confidence.

    "The aim is to give confidence to future generations, so that they grow up feeling more comfortable in their own skin –knowing that, regardless of looks, they are beautiful,” she said back in April. "I'm a woman with curves and I think these dresses are very flattering and pretty for women of all shapes."
