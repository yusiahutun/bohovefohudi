    Kim Kardashian’s latest Instagram photo dump isn’t for the faint of heart.

    Yesterday, the Skims mogul shared a new set of daring selfies that had her posing in thousands of dollars’ worth of Gucci clothes. These included a tan Gucci Supreme pajama set, featuring a slouchy shirt and baggy trousers completely covered in the Italian fashion house’s famous interlocking GG pattern, and a gobsmacking micro bra.

    The incredible lingerie piece, which is selling at $4,200, features tiny triangle-shaped panels made out of silver brass chainmail. On each panel is a large glittering GG adornment, with the sparkling crystals embellishing the interlocked letters.

    In the pictures, Kardashian sits on the floor at the foot of a bed in a dark room, where she lounges on a shaggy white rug. “It’s all Gucci,” she wrote in the caption.

    The risqué Instagram selfies capped off a week of fashionable appearances for the mom of four.

    Most recently, Kardashian appeared at Paris Fashion Week for Victoria Beckham’s Fall/Winter 2024 runway show, where she turned heads in a glossy pink slipdress with a slinky, body-hugging silhouette. She upped the ante with her accessories, adding a massive diamond choker necklace adorned with large drop crystal beads and a glittering cross-shaped pendant and a large diamond ring. For footwear, she opted for strappy nude heels.

    Prior to the Victoria Beckham show, she was spotted in Brooklyn wearing a cropped baby-pink tweed blazer with black trim and buttons, a vintage Chanel find. She paired the statement top with her signature skintight black spandex leggings, tucking the stretchy pants into a pair of pointy black velvet heeled boots from Balenciaga.
