    The former Spice Girl is the poster girl for transitional style

      Victoria Beckham showcased the best transitional outfit for spring – wearing the skirt of dreams! The wife of footballer David Beckham lit up the streets of Paris last week wearing a gold-toned yellow pleated skirt, a navy roll neck jumper and navy boots all from her own collection. The former Spice Girl is a great advert for mixing textures successfully and this dazzling outfit is a fab ensemble that will carry her through the seasons. The pleated skirt nails numerous trends in one – pleats, buttery hues and the hugely popular, on-trend midi length. She wore her trademark sleek brown hair in a slicked back ponytail and her must-have shades were placed firmly over her face. The mother-of-two had many outfit changes in the French Capital – the day before she looked incredible donning a tan ensemble which she teamed with black knee-high boots and a structured handbag. The caramel toned skirt and shirt combo had a fitted wraparound detail on her mid-section and highlighted her trim frame and minuscule waist.

      We love Victoria's pleated skirt

      It's been a very busy year for the fashion designer – not only has she showcased her upcoming collections during fashion week, but she has confirmed that she will soon be launching her very first skincare line. As reported by New Beauty, the mother of Brooklyn, 19, Romeo, 15, Cruz, 13, and Harper, six,  said: "I am currently in the process of creating my own colour, my own line of skincare creams, and a perfume. As a woman, I want to make the things I need in my life, the things that are missing."

      MORE: Victoria Beckham wears the most dreamy pastel suit and mint green shoes

      This isn’t Victoria's first foray into the extensive world of beauty – she also has a makeup range with cosmetic giants Estee Lauder. The 43-year-old launched the collaboration in 2016 and the 14-piece-collection includes primers, lipsticks, and eyeshadow and bronzing powders.
