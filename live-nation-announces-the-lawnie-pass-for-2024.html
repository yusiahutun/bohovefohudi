    Live Nation Announces The Lawnie Pass For 2024

    .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } @media(max-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 300px; height: 250px; } } @media(min-width: 800px) { .inarticle_banner_top { width: 728px; height: 90px; } }

    28 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Once again, Live Nation has revived their successful ‘Lawnie Pass‘ program, giving fans the opportunity to buy a season pass to their local amphitheater, ensuring access to a summer worth of concerts.

    For 2024, the Lawnie Pass will set fans back $239 including all fees, which for most of the participating list of venues, breaks down to about $10 dollars per show but the passes will be available only in limited quantities.

    Lawnie Pass will be on sale from February 7 at 10 a.m. PT through March 1 at lawniepass.com. Previous lawn pass purchasers will be able to participate in an exclusive presale, giving them first crack at the limited season passes.

    Additionally, each Lawnie Pass holder will receive a custom personalized credential that will serve as their ticket on each show day.

    Venues participating in Lawnie Pass include:

    Blossom Music Center (Cuyahoga Falls, OH)
    Coastal Credit Union Music Park at Walnut Creek (Raleigh, NC)
    Darien Lake Amphitheater (Darien Center, NY)
    Dos Equis Pavilion (Dallas, TX)
    Empower Federal Credit Union Amphitheater at Lakeview (Syracuse, NY)
    Freedom Mortgage Pavilion (Camden, NJ)
    Credit Union Amphitheatre (Tinley Park, IL)
    Hollywood Casino Amphitheatre – St. Louis (St. Louis, MO)
    Isleta Amphitheater (Albuquerque, NM)
    iTHINK Financial Amphitheatre (West Palm Beach, FL)
    Jiffy Lube Live (Bristow, VA)
    MIDFLORIDA Credit Union Amphitheatre (Tampa, FL)
    North Island Credit Union Amphitheatre (Chula Vista, CA)
    PNC Bank Arts Center (Holmdel, NJ)
    PNC Music Pavilion (Charlotte, NC)
    Ruoff Music Center (Noblesville, IN)
    RV Inn Style Resorts Amphitheater (Ridgefield, WA)
    Broadview Stage at SPAC (Saratoga Springs, NY)
    Shoreline Amphitheatre (Mountain View, CA)
    The Pavilion At Star Lake (Burgettstown, PA)
    Talking Stick Resort Amphitheatre (Phoenix, AZ)
    Toyota Amphitheatre (Wheatland, CA)
    Toyota Pavilion at Concord (Concord, CA)
    Utah First Credit Union Amphitheatre (West Valley City, UT)
    Veterans United Home Loans Amphitheater at Virginia Beach (Virginia Beach, VA)
    White River Amphitheatre (Auburn, WA)
    Xfinity Center (Mansfield, MA)
