    Neighbour of Sergeant Omondi Who Died in Chopper Crash Says He Couldn't Break Death News to His Wife

    The heartbroken neighbour of Sergeant Cliphonce Omondi, who passed away in a chopper crash in Elgeyo Markwet county has spoken. The neighbour, identified as Mosley on TikTok, was commenting on a post by Omondi's wife Tabitha Akinyi.

    24 Mar 2024, 00:00
    • TikTok user Mosley, Sergeant Cliphonce Omondi's neighbour, painfully disclosed he knew about his death
    • However, Mosley did not know how to break the shattering news to Omondi's wife, Tabitha Akinyi, when she asked him where her husband was
    • Akinyi said she knew about her husband's demise when asking Mosley about him, but she was in denial

    The heartbroken neighbour of Sergeant Cliphonce Omondi, who passed away in a chopper crash in Elgeyo Markwet county has spoken.

    The neighbour, identified as Mosley on TikTok, was commenting on a post by Omondi's wife Tabitha Akinyi.

    He disclosed he knew about the death of the military officer who died alongside nine other officers.

    Sergeant Cliphonce Omondi's neighbour speaks

    "You asked me, 'Where is my husband?' I took courage to pretend I knew nothing, but deep within my heart, I was bleeding, trying to play strong. I never returned because I lacked what to tell you ," he said.
