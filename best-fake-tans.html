    Best facial fake tan

    Tan-Luxe Super Glow Hyaluronic Self-Tan Serum

    Best facial fake tan

    Tan-Luxe Super Glow Hyaluronic Self-Tan Serum

    Now 20% Off

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    Tan-Luxe can always be looked to for the most innovative tanning-meets-skincare products. Perhaps the very best in the line, this clever facial tanner combines moisture-grabbing hyaluronic acid with low-level DHA, meaning it hydrates as it imparts a gentle, cumulative glow.

    Apply it to damp skin either morning or night: the result is a dewy complexion and subtle bronze tint that never veers into orange territory.

    Also worthy of a mention is the brilliant body iteration: Super Glow Body, which delivers light-as-air hydration alongside a subtle, skin-enhancing glow. It's ideal for anyone who prefers a lighter texture.

    Best fake tan mousse

    Amanda Harrington Illuminating Bronzing Mousse

    Best fake tan mousse

    Amanda Harrington Illuminating Bronzing Mousse

    Credit: Farfetch

    Amanda Harrington has placed an incredible level of thought into her eponymous tanning brand (unsurprising, for a woman famed for tanning A-list limbs). The line comprises prep products, foam formulas for the body and a liquid for the face, all infused with hyaluronic acid for a skincare boost (after all, hydration is essential to a flawless tan).

    But it’s the application process that really sets these products apart: true to Harrington’s in-salon treatments, each product should be applied with a large, dense buffing brush, rather than a flat (and often messy) mitt. The fluffy fibres allow you to really push the product into the skin, ensuring a totally seamless, streak-free result. It’s a lot easier than it may seem.

    Best luxury fake tan

    Sisley Paris Self Tanning Hydrating Body Skin Care

    Best luxury fake tan

    Sisley Paris Self Tanning Hydrating Body Skin Care

    Credit: SpaceNK

    Thick, creamy and beautifully scented, Sisley's iconic tanning lotion is one of the most indulgent around. The end result is glowier than most (thanks to the macadamia oil inside) and sun-starved limbs will feel deeply hydrated all day long. A classic.

    Best fake tan mist

    Oskia Adaptive Tan Mist

    Best fake tan mist

    Oskia Adaptive Tan Mist

    Credit: Feelunique

    Oskia's unique facial mist is a skincare treat first and a fake tan second: perfect for anyone who (quite rightly) scrutinises every product that meets their face. The 100 per cent natural (and colour-free) mist is loaded with skin-compatible vitamins and antioxidants alongside encapsulated natural DHA, which only make the resultant tan look even glowier. Mist it over clean skin – there's no need to rub or blend – making sure to include your neck and chest for the most believable result.

    Best fake tan for beginners

    St. Tropez Self Tan Classic Mousse

    Best fake tan for beginners

    St. Tropez Self Tan Classic Mousse

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    St. Tropez releases a host of novel new tanning formulas every summer, but the brand's classic mousse is the only one you really need to invest in.

    Pump the product into the palm of a tanning mitt, then buff it evenly into the skin using long, linear strokes. Quick-drying and non-sticky, it'll develop over the course of the day into a flattering, even tan.

    Best quick fake tan

    Bali Body 1 Hour Express Tan

    Best quick fake tan

    Bali Body 1 Hour Express Tan

    When you don't have all day to get ready for that big event, Bali Body's quick-developing tan is the perfect way to warm up your arms and legs in the time it takes to blow-dry your hair. While an instant tanner comes with the risk of streaking, this perfect middle-ground option can be buffed in and washed off in 60 minutes, leaving behind a moderate, even bronze.

    Garnier Summer Body Lotion

    Best gradual fake tan

    Garnier Summer Body Lotion

    Now 50% Off

    Credit: Boots

    If you're looking for a no-frills formula to deliver a gentle hint of warmth, Garnier's classic Summer Body tanning lotion really can't be beaten. It smells of apricots, feels richly hydrating, and doesn't leave limbs sticky. The colour is especially gentle, yet can be built up over the course of a week.

    best clear fake tan

    James Read Coconut Dry Oil

    best clear fake tan

    James Read Coconut Dry Oil

    Now 20% Off

    Credit: Feelunique

    A clear fake tan is ideal for anyone who doesn't want to wait before getting dressed, as there's no risk of transfer onto your clothing. This one is a 'dry' oil, meaning it absorbs quickly and gradually develops into a mid-level tan. The coconut scent is a mood-booster, too.

    Best fake tan for dry skin

    Nuxe Sun Hydrating Enhancing Self-Tan

    Credit: Feelunique

    Best fake tan for pale skin

    Isle of Paradise Self-Tanning Water

    Best fake tan for pale skin

    Isle of Paradise Self-Tanning Water

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    Isle of Paradise's original self-tanning waters come in three clever colour-correcting hues, so they'll impart a believable streak-free tan with no orangey tint on any skin tone. All three are transparent on application, so won't stain clothing or sheets, and can be simply misted over skin and buffed in with a mitt. They're ideal for nervous tanning newcomers.

    What's more, the brand has recently introduced eco-friendly refill pouches, meaning you can top up your bottle without purchasing more plastic.

    Omorovicza Glam Glow
