    I was honestly convinced Martha Stewart could no longer surprise me. She became part of this decade’s most unlikely duo, posed for the cover of Sports Illustrated Swimsuit at 81 years old, and went from homemaker to Insta baddie. Then, she wore a fall jacket I don’t think I’ve ever seen before, proving me entirely wrong.

    In a recent Instagram post, Martha Stewart posed in this season’s most surprising jacket: A short-sleeve puffer.

    Not only did the style surprise me, but I was also shocked by how much I loved it. While puffer vests have long been a staple, worn by everyone from Jennifer Garner to Rihanna, Stewart’s version brought an elevated touch to the practical piece. And we found her exact style, which you can grab at Amazon for just $45.

    Stewart was aptly wearing the Puffy Vest from her very own collection. While she opted for the mocha-colored one, this piece is also available in 22 other colors, including classics like black and navy, as well as a vibrant yellow and metallic silver. The puffer top, which features short sleeves, a zipper front, down filling, and fleece-lined pockets, boasts more than 1,500 five-star ratings at Amazon, where shoppers have deemed it “perfect for fall.”

    “[This] has just the right amount of warmth without being hot,” wrote one shopper who said that they’ve layered it with everything from a long-sleeved T-shirt to a turtleneck sweater. According to another person, “It is so lightweight to wear but warm,” noting that “the caps on the shoulders keep them warm and toasty.” “[It’s] much warmer than sleeveless [vests] and leaves arms more free for activities,” said a customer who added that the puffy collar is just right for a little extra coziness.” Another person loved that its lightweight fabric makes it “terrific” for travel, explaining that it can replace the “heavy coat” that takes up too much luggage space, and can even be worn in the airport without having to “worry about it being too warm.” 

    Also, it’s really damn cute. “Every time I wear [this], women stop me to compliment me on it,” said one fan who wrote that the puffer is both “warm and on-trend.” “I may live in this down vest,” said another person who described the color as “beautiful” and sizing and workmanship as “stunning.” And one other person called the short-sleeved vest “a breath of fresh air,” writing that they loved how “the cap sleeves and mandarin collar” brought something new to a piece that has “been the same…for decades.”

    A short-sleeved puffer vest is definitely not what I expected to top my fall wishlist but, alas, Stewart has done it again. Grab the piece shoppers love for $45 at Amazon.
