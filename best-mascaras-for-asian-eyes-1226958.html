    Factors to Consider When Buying the Best Mascara for Asian Eyes

    There is one thing that we all agree on – mascaras are one of the best products that will help your eyes to shine. It darkens and lifts your eyelashes, allowing them to look more enhanced and bring out the sparkle. So in case you’ve been thinking of investing in a good mascara, these factors can help make a more informed choice. 

    Ease of Application: As is the case with any kind of makeup product, the easier a mascara is to apply, the better it is for the wearer. This way, you don’t have to avoid any awkwardness but you can also ensure that no product is wasted. 

    Ingredients: Makeup is all about effortlessly enhancing your natural features. So for this, you need to be more research-oriented. Find out the ingredients that suit your eyes while also soothing them. The best ones will give you your desired effect too.  

    Easy Removal: A good mascara should be one that’s equally easy to apply as well as remove. Mostly look for products that can be easily washed off or don’t spread further. They should easily come off with the help of a cleansing balm or a facial cleanser. 

    Overall Result: While trial and error is always the name of the game, the same applies when choosing mascara. Look closely at how slowly the product dries up as well as how it lengthens your lashes and the amount of time it holds the curls for.

    How to Use the Best Mascara for Asian Eyes?

    Here is a list of quick and easy steps to get the best out of using your mascara: 

    Step 1 - If possible, clean your lashes so that the product can go on smoothly. 

    Step 2 - Another smart but optional tip is to get a lash curler and set your lashes in the form that you would like them to be before applying the product to them. 

    Step 3 - Use the brush as a wand and start from the roots of your eyelashes and slowly yet steadily lengthen the product.

    Step 4 - You can also do small zigzag motions across the lashes to slowly pull out the brush in a way that adds more volume to the length and curl.

    Step 5 - Let your mascara dry a bit before checking how it looks. Then, apply a second layer or a third depending on your preference.

    FAQ's

    What mascara is good for Asian eyelashes?

    Generally speaking, when it comes to Asian eyelashes, a mascara that can help you with lifting and curling is considered to be good.

    What makes Asian mascara different?

    Asian mascara is manufactured to target the issue of monolid, which involves straight or droopy eyelashes. The formula helps to separate, lengthen and curl those eyelashes while holding them in a long-lasting manner.

    How do Asians keep their eyelashes curled?

    Some ways that Asians keep their eyelashes curled are by using eyelash curlers daily, using water to wet eyelashes, or even using a lash primer as well as finding a good mascara with a formula that holds their eyelash curls throughout the day.
