    She didn't take home the trophy on Dancing With The Stars but former contestant Chelsea Kane is definitely a winner when it comes to love - and beach bodies.

    The third place contestant in the last season of the reality show revealed her fabulous bikini body as she frolicked in the ocean with boyfriend Stephen Colletti in Mexico today.

    The young couple are currently enjoying a romantic trip for two and despite all the other beauties on the beach, smitten Laguna Beach star Colletti only had eyes for Kane.

    Young love: Chelsea Kane and Stephen Colletti frolicked in the ocean together today as they enjoyed a romantic holiday in Mexico

    The 22-year-old  Disney darling met Colletti, 25, on a blind date and they've been inseparable ever since.

    'We were set up on a blind date by my manager,' Kane told American magazine Us Weekly in April.

    'He is pretty cute, he's a really good guy, we've been having fun together.'

    Hand-in-hand: Kane and Colletti were set up on a blind date by her manager earlier this year

    The former teen star admits that she had a crush on Colletti during his MTV days. 'She tore his photo out of a magazine to show friends!' a source told the magazine.

    The instant attraction was mutual, as One Tree Hill star Colletti gushed about Kane's performances on the reality dancing competition.

    'She's great. I'm so proud of her. She's doing amazing on 'Dancing with the Stars,' he told Us Weekly during the competition.

    Hot couple: Colletti dated Laguna Beach stars Kristen Cavallari and Lauren Conrad in the past but now he is serious with Kane

    Sexy in the sand: The couple took time about from their romantic walk on the beach to snuggle in the sand

    'We're having a great time together. She's a great girl.'

    Colletti has previously dated fellow MTV stars Lauren Conrad and Kristen Cavallari, who is now engaged to NFL star Jay Cutler.

    Kane and her dancing partner Mark Ballas came in third place on Dancing With The Stars last season behind Kirstie Alley and champion Hines Ward, despite consistently coming in top of the leader board for numerous weeks.

    Dancer's body: Like most contestants on Dancing With the Stars, Kane lost weight on the show

    The pair heated things up off the dance floor when the Disney star appeared in Ballas' steamy video, Hotwire, which featured them cuddling in bed together.

    After the finale in May, she donated her dancing shoes from her final spin on the show to Planet Hollywood in New York City.

    This way: Stephen reaches out a hand to Chlsea while she looks apprehensive

    Off skiing? Stephen and Chelsea chat as he prepares to get on a jetski

    'It’s awesome,' she told OK! magazine in the U.S. 'I mean, I’m glad to donate them, and also to never have to wear them again, because it was a very painful experience.

    'My feet will never be the same. Those heels are not easy to dance in!'
