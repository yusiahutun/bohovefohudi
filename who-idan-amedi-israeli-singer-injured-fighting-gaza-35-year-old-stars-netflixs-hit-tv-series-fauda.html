    Idan Amedi is an Israeli singer-songwriter and actor

    Amedi is an Israeli singer and actor. He was born in Pisgat Ze’ev, Jerusalem, in 1988. Per his biography, he released his eponymous debut album in 2011.

    He was in Netflix’s hit war drama Fauda

    Besides music, Amedi is also known for his TV career. Per CNN, he joined the cast of Fauda in 2017. The show follows the story of a “top Israeli agent who comes out of retirement to hunt for a Palestinian fighter he thought he’d killed” according to its Netflix page.

    Who is Idan Amedi married to?

    According to Sportskeeda, Amedi has been married to Miriam Binyaminov since 2018. The same source states that she works as a social activist, catering to people with disabilities.
