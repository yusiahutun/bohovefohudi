    Jennifer Koester Named President Of Business Operations At Sphere Entertainment

    18 Jun 2024, 00:00

    NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Sphere Entertainment Co., operators of The Sphere in Las Vegas, announced the hire of Jennifer Koester for the role of President, Sphere Business Operations effective February 5th.

    In her new role at Sphere Entertainment, Koester will oversee strategy and execution of the company’s business operations, including calendar and ticket sales for the company’s proprietary shows and attractions. She will also work on developing strategic partnerships, focusing on exclusive bookings for the high-tech venue with artists, managers, and promoters, as well as developing a corporate conference business for product launches and other events.

    Koester will be based in New York and report directly to James Dolan, Executive Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Sphere Entertainment.

    She joins Sphere Entertainment with a wealth of experience across sales, marketing, technology, and digital business. Her past senior roles include stints at Google and Cablevision.

    Ms. Koester received a J.D. from St. John’s University School of Law and a bachelor’s degree in management information system and services from Binghamton University.

    “I am pleased to welcome Jennifer to Sphere Entertainment,” said Mr. Dolan. “We have seen a strong worldwide reaction to Sphere from customers, brands and artists since our September opening, and we look forward to leveraging Jennifer’s extensive background driving growth at premier technology and entertainment brands as we continue to build our Sphere business.”

    “The opportunity to join the Sphere team and be part of this next-generation entertainment medium is a tremendous opportunity,” said Ms. Koester. “Throughout my career, I have focused on pioneering and delivering growth strategies that bridge business needs and leverage new technologies, and I am excited to bring that experience to Sphere.”
