    Nicole Richie Looks Sleek and Mysterious in All-Black Saint Laurent Look

    Nicole Richie is really mastering the art of the monochromatic fit. A week ago, she attended the premiere of her new Netflix film, Dont Tell Mom the Babysitters Dead, in an intricate Schiaparelli gown with matching heels. Now, the socialite has pulled out yet another sleek black ensemble.

    23 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Nicole Richie is really mastering the art of the monochromatic fit. A week ago, she attended the premiere of her new Netflix film, Don’t Tell Mom the Babysitter’s Dead, in an intricate Schiaparelli gown with matching heels. Now, the socialite has pulled out yet another sleek black ensemble.

    On Tuesday, while making a few stops to promote the new comedy, the Simple Life alum was seen wearing an all-black outfit courtesy of Saint Laurent by Anthony Vaccarello. At the center of the look was a backless knit bodysuit with a halter-style neckline, which she paired with a high-waisted pencil skirt to create the illusion that she was wearing a little black dress. To really sell it as a singular piece, she added a patent leather belt across her waist, which featured a shiny golden buckle and a square enamel insert.

    MEGA//Getty Images

    Accessories-wise, Richie kept the inky theme going, with a pair of large cat-eye sunglasses, square-stiletto pumps, and black tights. She also wore an oversize jacket in lambskin, which she kept off her shoulders and wrapped around her arms, somehow leaning further into the super-spy effect of it all. Richie also added a pair of dangly golden earrings crafted in geometric shapes.

    The actor was seen wearing the fit as she left The Drew Barrymore Show yesterday morning, and she wore the same look later last night, when she visited The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon (but dropped the jacket and sunglasses).
