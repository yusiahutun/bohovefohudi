    Rich Dollaz’s Short Wiki: The Story That Made Richard Trowers Rich Dollaz

    Rich Dollaz was born with the real name of Richard Trowers in Waterbury, Connecticut in 1975. Dollaz celebrates his birthday on the 4th of August. He was born to African and American parents and is of black ethnicity and American nationality.

    He was never a music mogul to start with, nor did he hail from a family with extensive connections in the industry. He began as a rookie at Bad Boy Records and worked his way to the top. His success is reflected on the artists like Yung Joc, Cheri Dennis, and Gorilla Zoe who he helped establish. 

    Later, in 2009, he started his own management company “Dollaz Unlimited” that became the center of career development for up-and-coming artists. The company is well known for representing musicians, models, media personalities, actors, and authors.

    Besides his works as a manager, he is also known for his vast connections in the music industry. It's no wonder his successful career earned him a net worth of $2.5 million. 

    Rich Dollaz: Dating Is His Game

    Rich Dollaz, the music mogul, has unmistakable eyes for talent and pretty women he seeks to add to his dating list. Thanks to his appearance on the VH1’s Love & Hip Hop: New York, the number on his dating list soared.

    His joining the show fostered his dating desires: he kicked off his relationship with Erica Mena, a beauty he met on the show, in 2012. However, the relationship ended in 2013.

    Soon, in 2014, rumors about his involvement with Jhonni Blaze, another participant of the show, started floating in. Dollaz did not say anything regarding the rumor, but, given his nature, we would be surprised if it was not true. 

    Dollaz never knew the stop for his dating games. Moniece Slaughter, the singer and reality star, became his girlfriend in 2015 when she was a part of the show Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood. The couple’s relationship did not last long either, as they split before the end of 2015.

    In 2016, Dollaz's affair was with Jade Watson, a former model, and paralegal. Though the duo has not provided much information about their breakup, it is speculated that the couple has already separated.

    Dollaz’s recent events show a resumption of the romance with his long-term on and off girlfriend, Erica Mena. His recent Instagram post with Erica Mena maybe a hint of reconciliation with the ex.

    Well, one thing is for sure: Rich Dollaz is a dreamboat of many. Known for his high-profile relationships with women, he has left no space for the rumors of being gay. 

    Rich Dollaz's Kids: Are They All From the Same Mother?

    With such big scores on romance, no wonder, Rich Dollaz has—not one, not two, but—four children: two sons and two daughters. His daughter Ashley also appeared on the show, Love & Hip Hop: New York, with him.

    Ashley’s mother, Miracle is less known and not talked about in the media. Even though Dollaz keeps a low profile about his kids unlike his dating life, his son was seen on his Instagram not long ago.

    He sure shares the looks of his father.

    Dollaz’s another daughter, Christiana Nicolle popped up in the media amidst the legal affairs Dollaz had to face. Dollaz's previous fling with Chaundrea Nicolle, who happens to be the baby mama of Dollaz’s daughter, Christiana, ended badly with definite bad scars for Chaundrea.

    She filed a lawsuit against Dollaz for owing her some serious cash in child support he ignored to pay: $188,000 to be exact. It led to an active warrant out for his arrest as of April 20, 2015.

    Chaundrea said that Rich has not paid any child support, and has even denied that the child is his. However, no further news about how the lawsuit ended is available. 

    As for his another son, Dollaz has been low profile and has managed to flow the secrecy all along.

    There is no denying that Dollaz is the king of hearts—both his dating life and fan base vouch for that.
