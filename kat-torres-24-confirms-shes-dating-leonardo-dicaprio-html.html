    He's never been one to hang around between relationships, and is rarely seen without a stunning model on his arm.

    And now, after splitting from Victoria's Secret model Toni Garrn earlier this year, Leonardo DiCaprio's latest love interest has been identified as 24-year-old international model Kat Torres, who works for Elite Model management in Paris. 

    Blonde Torres, a former 2012 Miss Caieiras beauty queen, a small city in Sao Paulo, spoke to Brazilian newspaper Extra confirming that she is dating The Great Gatsby star.

    'Leo's amazing!' Model Kat Torres has confirmed she is dating Leonardo DiCaprio and opened up about their romance

    She said: 'I met Leo in Cannes. We were staying in the same house at the festival.'

    New love: Rarely a month goes by that Leonardo doesn't have a new model girlfriend on his arm

    The pair were photographed together, dressed casually and surrounded by security as their pushed their way through crowds.

    However, it appears that the model whose real name is Katiuscia Torres Soares, who was born in Belem in the northern state of Para, is a bit of a novice to the paparazzi as she claims surprise at being photographed with the Hollywood superstar.

    She said: 'I really don’t know how the photographers got the picture because we were well guarded and no one could come close in any circumstances.'

    While confirming that she is seeing DiCaprio, the model shied clear of saying too much about the romance.

    She said: 'Leo is amazing, but I worry about what he will think about me talking to you. We have a pact that nothing can ever be said about our relationship.

    'In Europe it is different. People see us together in many places so they do not need to ask us about anything.'

    Torres who is 1’57m tall also revealed that DiCaprio is protective of his privacy, adding: 'He doesn’t like being photographed and barely goes out walking once people have realized it's him.'

    Just peachy: Kat is a 24-year-old model who has enjoyed international success

    DiCaprio, 38, was last seen with Toni Garrn at the US Open Tennis Championships in early September, but it seems that relationship may have already fizzled out and he’s found another muse in the Brazilian beauty who is 14 years his junior.

    The Los Angeles-born actor’s flit from beauty to beauty maybe a sign that he still hasn’t found the one to settle down with.

    He dated Brazilian model Gisele Bündchen on-and-off for four years, and split from Israeli model Bar Refaeli in 2011 after nearly six years together.

    But a source close to DiCaprio told Mail Online that he is not dating Torres and her story is 'completely false'.
