    Are you interested in working for Kenya Airways (KQ)? Would you like to grow or start your career with the company? If that is the case, then you should be well versant with the Kenya Airways recruitment portal.

    Kenya Airways is a member of Sky Team Alliance and a leading airline that covers over 51 destinations across the world. Its fleet comprises of some of the youngest airplanes in Africa. With 32 aircraft, the company needs a lot of staff members to work as pilots, cabin crew and air hostess.

    Kenya Airways recruitment portal application guide

    As a top company, KQ knows that its greatest asset of all is people. When employed, you are provided with training, tools, and systems you need to perform. You are also equipped with a pleasant work environment that will ensure you yield your full potential. Hence, this is one of the best employers in the country.

    You will need the following to access the Kenya Airways career website and apply for any vacancies:

    • Internet connection
    • Laptop/desktop
    • Basic computer skills
    • A browser
    • Your ID number
    • Your KCSE grades

    Follow the procedure below to search for any open positions such as Kenya Airways internship.

    Part 1: Registration

    Use the procedure below to sign up:

  • Connect to the internet: There are various ways you can ensure that your gadget can access the web. You can use a LAN cable, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or USB tethering. The faster the source, the less time you will take to complete the procedure.
  • Open your browser: We recommend using Firefox, Safari, Google Chrome, or Opera.
  • Go to the KQ careers website.
  • Accept the privacy agreement terms: You can do so by clicking on the green “I Accept” button.
  • Login/register: If you have an account, enter your credentials to log in. If you don’t click on the “Register Today” link.
  • Fill in the required details: You should key in your User Name, Password, and email address. The fields with an asterisk are mandatory, so you should fill them in.
  • Click on the “Register”: Once you have filled the above information. Click on this red button, as shown below.

    Part 2: Completing your profile

    Follow the procedure below:

  • Enter your details: Once you are logged in, click on the “My Account” tab then fill all the necessary information.
  • Key in your employment history and education qualifications: You should only enter the correct details. You might also add any other aptitude you think might be a plus on the “Qualifications and Skills” tab. You must include your KCSE mean grade and Mathematics, English, and Physics scores when applying for job vacancies at Kenya Airways. Type this in the spaces provided.
  • Indicate your national ID number: Do not confuse this with the Serial Number. This is usually located on the front top right side of your ID card.
  • Save the information: Go through what you have typed and make sure it is correctly done. You can then proceed with this step.

    Part 3: Application

    Once you have successfully saved all your information, it now time to apply for Kenya Airways jobs for form four leavers, air hostess, and even pilot.

  • Specify the job posting date: Recent vacancies might be the best for you.
  • Tag the location: Choose the country you wish to work from and also the city.
  • Tick the vacancy name: There are over 20 fields you can work in.
  • Indicate your experience: You can either be experienced or having a standard knowledge.
  • Click the “Apply” button: Once you spot any vacancy you want, click on this option, which is usually green in colour and next to the job title.
  • Complete the application: There are some four simple stages you will be prompted to undergo. Fill any required details.

    • Note that any Kenya Airways cabin crew recruitment applicant should meet the minimum requirements. Having a higher qualification is an added advantage. You should not waste your time to apply for any position you are not fit for.

    What if I have previously applied for a vacancy?

    In the event that you are not successful in your application, especially as a Kenya Airways pilot, the company will communicate to you about what you need to do in order to increase your chances of being successful next time around. Due to a large number of applications that the Kenya Airways receives, only shortlisted candidates who were contacted will be given feedback on their applications
