    Ben Barnes Bio

    The actor was born on August 20, 1981, by Tricia and Thomas Barnes under the birth name Benjamin Thomas Barnes. He is the first child of his parents and has only one sibling, who is his brother Jack Barnes. Ben’s early education took place at the Homefield Preparatory School in Sutton and at King’s College School in Wimbledon. He also holds a BA Honours from Kingston University, where he studied Drama and English Literature.

    Ben became interested in entertainment as a teenager when he started playing in musical theaters, and at fifteen he got his first big job as a drummer for the musical remake of Bugsy Malone. In 2006, Ben began his acting career by appearing in the series Doctors, where he was cast as Craig Unwin, and the following year, 2007, he was given his most popular role as Prince Caspian in the series The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian.

    Over the years, the actor has been given roles in the films Simple Virtue, Dorian Gray, Locked Up, The Chronicles of Narnia: The Journey of the Dawn Kicker, Killing Bono, The Words, The Great Wedding, By the Gun, Jackie & Ryan and the epic film Seventh Son.

    The actor has also appeared in sitcoms such as Split Decision, Sons of Liberty, Exposed, Westworld, where he is still cast as Logan Delos, and in the ongoing series The Punisher, where he plays the title role of Billy Russo.
