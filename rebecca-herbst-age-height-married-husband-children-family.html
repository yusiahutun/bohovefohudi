    Who Is Rebecca Ann Herbst? Bio & Wiki

    Rebecca Herbst was born as Rebecca Ann Herbst on 12th May 1977, in Encino, California, United States. Moreover, she is American by her nationality and belongs to Caucasian white ethnicity. She was raised by her mother, Debbie, and her father, Wayne Herbst. Herbst grew up along with her elder sister, Jennifer Herbst.

    During her childhood, Herbst had a keen interest in figure skating and participated in many competitions as a competitive player. Later, she found her true love in acting and decided to concentrate on acting to become a successful actress.

    Inside Of Rebecca Herbst’s Career

    Rebecca Herbst started her career from the American television legal drama series, L.A. Law playing the role of Jennifer Simmons in 1986. Later, she appeared as a young, Gail in the drama series, Highway to Heaven in 1987. Herbst also voiced as Rennee’s friend in the animated series, McGee and Me! in 1989. Furthermore, she worked with Jewel Staite in Space Cases.

    Herbst made her appearance in the American comedy movie, Why Me? where she worked under the direction of Gene Quintano in 1990. Furthermore, she featured as Emily in the made-for-television movie, Kaleidoscope. She then featured in the biographical drama movie, Hefner: Unauthorized as Barbi Benton in 1999. Her other television works are Beverly Hills, 90210, Step by Step, etc.

    How Much is Rebecca Herbst’s Net Worth?

    Rebecca Herbst has an estimated net worth of $3 Million as of 2019. Having such an impressive wealth, she also collects a lucrative fortune from her other works which include advertisements, endorsements, cameo appearances, etc. She earns an annual salary in the range of $450 thousand to $960 thousand.

    Rebecca is a fashion designer and designed many amazing clothes. She also designs most of her Daytime Emmy dresses. She further designed clothes for her co-stars, Natalia Livingston and Kimberly McCullough for the 33rd Daytime Emmy Awards ceremony.

    Other than this, Herbst became a spokeswoman for Purpose Skincare in 2007. She also donates a fruitful wealth to the Smile Train charity.

    Rebecca Herbst’s Personal Life: Married, Husband, Children

    Rebecca Herbst first met her husband, Michael Saucedo, an actor on the set of General Hospital. Shortly the duo expressed their feelings for each other and began dating.

    The couple married on 1st June 2001, in Encino, California. Together, they share three kids, Ethan, born on 31st October 2001, Ella Bailey, born on 12th April 2004 and Emerson Truett, born on 9th August 2010.

    Before her marriage with Michael, Herbst dated an Australian actor, Ingo Rademacher. However, they split up due to some misunderstandings. Later, she was linked with Johnny Lindersmith, a photographer in the late 1990s.

    On 30th December 1999, they engaged. Unfortunately, they called off their engagement citing irreconcilable differences. Currently, Herbst is living happily with her spouse and kids with no signs and rumors of divorce and affairs.
