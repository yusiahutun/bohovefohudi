    Reading the Bible should be every Christian's hobby, but this is seldom the case. The major reason why most Christians do not read the Bible frequently is that they lack a proper spiritual foundation. Such a foundation can only be established through prayer because without doing so, it will be a war the devil will always win. The role of prayers before reading the Bible is to fortify you against any attacks by the devil.

    Faith grows by following the teachings of Christ, but you cannot know these teachings if you don't read the Bible. Saying a simple prayer before reading the Bible will usher in the Holy Spirit to guide you in reading, understanding, and applying the word of God. This will help you avoid misinterpreting the Bible and going against the wishes of the Lord.

    Prayers before reading the Bible

    Before starting your daily readings, begin with prayer. God listens to different kinds of prayers no matter your situation. The various prayer categories help Christians choose the right type of prayers and say them.

    Opening prayer for Bible study

    The perfect prayer before reading or studying the Bible is the one in which you ask God to be in your midst as you engage in the exercise. It should focus on the theme of the specific book or chapter you intend to study. Such prayers can be as simple as:

    Sabbath school opening prayers for worship or church service

    Almighty Father, thank you for the opportunity you have given us to learn your word. Make us not take for granted your goodness. We feel blessed that you have allowed us to hear your loving voice through this Bible study. It teaches us to walk more closely with you and to follow the path you have chosen for us. We pray for hearts that are responsive to your commands which you give us through the Bible. We ask for these things in your loving name. Amen.

    Catholic prayer before reading the Bible

    Like most other denominations, Catholics pray before they read the Bible. The aim is to thank God for his word and seek his guidance in understanding and spreading it. This can be achieved through a simple prayer before reading the Bible like:

    Dear Lord, I hope that I will know, love, and understand you better through reading your word. Let your spirit guide me in applying your word to my life. May the Holy Spirit enlighten my mind and enkindle a greater love for you and others in my heart. Amen.

    Prayers for Bible Sunday

    Bible Sunday is significant because it gives Christians the opportunity to come together to study the bible. God will inspire such a gathering when they start and begin the event with a word of prayer. Examples of effective prayers for Bible Sunday include:

    Loving Father, usher your blessings into this meeting of your believers. Speak to all our hearts through your holy word. Bless us with the knowledge to understand the scriptures we read, that we may leave more refreshed than we came. Use us to spread your teachings to those most in need of them. In Jesus' name, we pray, Amen.

    Dear Father, we humble ourselves in prayer before we start reading the Bible. We understand that we would not be able to accomplish our plans for this meeting without your presence. Therefore, send your grace and divine wisdom to our Bible study group to have a successful meeting. Amen.

    Before Jesus died, he promised to send a helper in the form of the Holy Spirit. The Holy Spirit acts as a protector, so Christians should pray for guidance and protection before reading the Bible. Below are prayers to the Holy Spirit before reading the Bible:

    Oh Holy Spirit, descend upon our hearts and minds. Inflame them with the fire of your divine love. Dear Lord, with the help of the Holy Spirit, let your holy word inspire and instruct our hearts to emulate the teachings of your word. Through Christ, we believe and pray. Amen.
