    This week marked the beginning of the most exciting time of the year for the red carpet as the Golden Globes kicked off the 2023 Hollywood award season. The event saw plenty of the world's biggest stars in attendance as the award show returned to TV screens after a one-year hiatus. This included Ana de Armas – who was nominated for her role in Blonde, where she portrayed Marilyn Monroe. She turned heads in a beautiful intricate design by Louis Vuitton.

    The actress – who is no stranger to a little Hollywood glamour – wore a strapless bustier gown in a striking black and white. The dress was created in two pieces; there was a structured front panel that had been embroidered with 3D sequins in a geometric pattern, while the back of the dress was made in pleated black silk with a train, which gave a beautiful fluidity to the gown.

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Related Story

    The dress was custom-made for de Armas especially for the event by the Louis Vuitton team. Over 150 hours went into the making of the dress, where 25 metres of silk and 800 sequins were used, and she accessorised with the most beautiful diamond-and-emerald bangle from the Louis Vuitton High Jewellery Liberty collection. She kept the rest of her ensemble simple, leaving her neckline bare, and opted for a super-smooth blow-dry which framed her face.
