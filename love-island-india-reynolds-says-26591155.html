    Stunning Love Island star India Reynolds has launched her latest range with Pour Moi, with a series of dazzling lingerie snaps to match.

    India has modelled her newest range herself and has already got her fans up in arms after sharing a sneak-peek at some parts of the collection on Instagram.

    Just last week the brunette beauty posed in the white lingerie set and put her curves on full display as she sat on the beach and basked in the sun - leaving fans utterly speechless.

    The starlet teased the collection as she told her followers it was coming "very soon", prompting an influx of compliments from her loyal legion of followers.

    And it looks like the stunning undies snaps will continue to come over the next few weeks, with the Pour Moi officially launching - something which India is incredibly proud of.

    Speaking exclusively to Daily Star, she said: "I love all the bright colours of this collection, we've still got the classic black but I love the bright and sexy pieces. It's such an incredible fit, it's perfect for bigger sizes and we're hoping to go even bigger, so everyone can wear it.

    "Being inclusive is so important to me. Obviously some sets will be better for those with smaller boobs because of the strap sizes, we're trying to find the fine line trying to get super sexy and super supportive."

    It's not just the bedroom that India loves the collection for though, with the star confessing: "I love wearing underwear as outerwear.

    "Styling a longline bra with a blazer over the top - it's my favourite. I love seeing how it can all go together to create an outfit."

    India's underwear snaps don't just make it to her Instagram page though, as she also loves her OnlyFans site and the content she shares.

    Her site is filled with her own snaps, cooking videos and styling hauls too.

    India said: "I think it's really empowering. I feel like that is the way that social media is moving. If you really like someone, rather than just looking at everything they do for free you can choose to pay for it.

    "You can support the actual person and what they're trying to do. I think being able to create exactly what I want is empowering."

    India said that her content which is more costly will be what she's more likely to share on OnlyFans over Instagram.

    She added that her OnlyFans has become more of a "community" and "more personal", so she isn't as picky with what goes on her OnlyFans.

    Her loyal subscribers have helped India rack up 34,000 likes on the platform from over 500 uploads - and her love of the site suggests that's only going to grow.
