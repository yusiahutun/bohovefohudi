    Love Island star Chloe Burrows put on an eye-popping display as she went braless underneath a sheer ensemble on Wednesday (July 5).

    The reality star, 27, who catapulted to fame after appearing on series seven of the ITV2 dating show in 2021, pulled out all the stops as she attended the Digital Broadcast Awards in London.

    Leaving little to the imagination, the blonde bombshell posed up a storm in the lace pink jumpsuit which boasted cut-outs across her chest held together with diamante buttons.

    Chloe added height to her frame with a pair of red strappy heels and accessorised with chunky silver earrings, carrying her belongings in a dazzling clutch bag.

    She styled her platinum tresses in a high bun, leaving sections loose to frame her face.

    The podcast host looked ultra glam as she applied heavy lashings of makeup, including a nude lipstick and pink eyeshadow to perfectly compliment her outfit.

    "So much fun at the Digital Broadcast Awards last night with my @channel4.0 family!," Chloe captioned the post.

    The TV star's slew of sizzling snaps attracted plenty of attention from her friends and followers who raced to the comments to shower her with compliments.

    Chloe's Love Island co-star Liberty Poole gushed: "Love this outfit and whole look," while Georgia Townend shared: "You look so goooooddddd".

    "Stunning is there anything u cant pull off?," penned a third person as another swooned: "Looking super hot chloe".

    The ITV2 star was announced as part of the line-up for the new series of Celebs Go Dating which recently began filming at Morden Hall in South London.

    Alongside Chloe, the upcoming series will feature former Love Island star Adam Collard, Made in Chelsea's Mark-Francis Vandelli, TV presenter Vanessa Feltz, model Lottie Moss and comedian Spuddz.
