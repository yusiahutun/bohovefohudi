    Old version act of contrition prayer by the Catholic Church

    The Catholic Church doctrine highlights four essential elements of Christian prayer, one of them being the prayer of contrition/repentance. The prayers are already composed, and Catholics worldwide repeat them word by word. There are a dozen acts of contrition prayers by the Catholic Church that you can use as you repent.

    Traditional catholic act of contrition prayer

    Oh my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended Thee, and I detest all my sins because of Thy just punishments, but most of all, because they offend Thee, my God, who art all-good and deserving of all my love. With the help of Thy grace, I firmly resolve to sin no more and avoid in the near occasion of sin. Amen.
