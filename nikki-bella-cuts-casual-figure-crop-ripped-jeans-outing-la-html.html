    Nikki had an on-off relationship with WWE heartthrob John Cena but their engagement broke up last summer.

    Her outing comes just days after she finally decided to call her latest flame Artem Chigvintsev, 37, her 'boyfriend.' 

    Casual: The Total Divas star sported a grey off the shoulder t-shirt which she tied into a knot, displaying her toned torso

    'We're in a relationship,' said Artem, who has featured on the reality shows Strictly Come Dancing and Dancing With The Stars.

    Nikki and Artem were partnered up on Dancing With The Stars back in 2017, but at the time she was still in a relationship with John.  

    Given the unique way in which they met, it was only fitting the pair announced the news with fans and expressed their love through a choreographed dance. 

    Style: The former wrestler also donned a pair of ripped blue jeans and added height to her frame with a pair of heels

    The movement piece was in black and white and color with Rita Ora's Let You Love Me playing in the background.

    She said during the podcast it reminded her of the 'journey' she and her new beau had been on so far.

    I'm the one that chose the song and [it’s] kind of my journey with Artem and dating,' she explained.

    'I felt myself falling for him a lot but then I felt like I was acting out in ways,' she said, adding: 'I was avoiding it.'
