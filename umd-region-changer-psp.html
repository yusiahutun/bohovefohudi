    While PSP games are region free, some UMD Movies are unfortunately region locked. But don't worry, this awesome UMD Region Changer app can bypass that restriction for you!

    Installation

  • Run UMD Region Changer.
  • Choose the region.
  • Once XMB display insert UMD of you selected Region.
  • Play and enjoy your Movie.

    • NOTE: This is not persistant, if you restart your PSP the UMD Drive will default back to its original state.
