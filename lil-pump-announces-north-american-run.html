    Lil Pump Announces North American Run

    11 Jul 2024, 00:00

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Soundcloud sensation Lil Pump announced he’s hitting the road in 2019 for a tour of North America.

    The tour launches on Friday, April 19 at WaMu Theater in Seattle, Washington, and hits major markets across North America for the next month before it concludes on Friday, May 22 at The Shrine Expo Hall in Los Angeles, California.

    Lil Pump will be joined for the run by “Red Roses” rapper Lil Skies, who will appear as a special guest for the tour.

    Lil Pump will be on the road in support of his sophomore offering Harverd Dropout which was released on February 22nd via Tha Lights Global/Warner Bros. Records.

    Lil Skies is riding high on the success of his major label debut mixtape Life Of A Dark Rose [All We Got Ent/Atlantic Records] has been streamed over a billion times since it was released last January.

    Lil Pump’s latest single ‘Be Like Me’
