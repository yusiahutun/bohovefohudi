    Barack Obama 'ready to call Drake' over Kanye's comment 'vulgar comments' about Michelle

    Kanye West isnt one to hold back on his thoughts, and hes now shown his interest in Michelle Obama in a rather crude way. From running for President to wanting their former Presidents wife, we cant keep up.

    22 May 2024, 00:00

    Kanye West isn’t one to hold back on his thoughts, and he’s now shown his interest in Michelle Obama in a rather crude way. From running for President to wanting their former President’s wife, we can’t keep up.

    The music star is happily married to Bianca Censori but if he had the option to add someone else into the mix, he’s revealed it would be none other than Michelle Obama. Fans are now joking Barack may need to call Drake for a diss track (who Kanye West has ‘slated’ once again, may we add).

    No question is ever off the cards in a Ye interview, as he was recently asked who he would add into the mix in the bedroom with his wife on The Download podcast.

    After some hesitating, the Touch the Sky rapper confidential responded by saying, “Michelle Obama,” as he and host Justin Laboy burst into laughter.

    “Gotta f*** the President’s wife,” the celebrity said before laughing even harder. “I don’t think that’s the legit answer, but that’s the answer for today,” the host laughed.

    Of course, Michelle or Barack have not responded to the comments, but fans on X, formerly known as Twitter have been making jokes about how the former POTUS would react.

    “LOL Barack Obama about to call Drake for a diss track,” wrote one. For those of you who may be unaware, Drake has become the ‘King’ of diss tracks lately, recently dropping Push Ups, replying to stars Kendrick Lamar, Future, Metro Boomin, Rick Ross, and The Weeknd, to name a few. He then released Taylor Made Freestyle, as a follow-up.

    Yep, you guessed it Kanye found a way to hop onto the beef, dropping a remix to Like That, by Future, Metro Boomin, and Kendrick, who ‘dissed’ Drake and J Cole on the original.

    Kanye ‘inserts himself’ into Drake beef

    It’s no surprise West hopped on, as he and Drake haven’t had the smoothest of relationships. In his remix, Ye raps the lines: “Where’s Lucian? Serve your master/ You caught a little bag for your masters, didn’t ya/ Lifetime deal, I feel bad for n****. “Y’all so outta sight, outta mind/ I can’t even think of a Drake line.”

    The lyrics are seemingly referencing the God’s Plan rapper’s record deal with Universal music, suggesting he’s sold the rights to his music for money.

    “[Future] called me and I went to the studio and laid that. We went through the creative process of adding the chords and called the Hooligans out in London to get on the joint,” Ye claimed on The Download podcast.

    “Everybody was very, very energized about the elimination of Drake.”

    We’re sure this ‘beef’ isn’t going to die down anytime soon.
