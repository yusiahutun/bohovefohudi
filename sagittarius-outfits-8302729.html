    The last fire sign of the zodiac, Sagittarius is adventurous in life and fashion. With Sagittarius season quickly approaching — it runs from November 22 through December 21 — now is the perfect time to learn how to craft a statement-making outfit. To help us break down the ins and outs of Sagittarius style, InStyle tapped Taryn Bond, an evolutionary-intuitive astrologer.

    “As a mutable sign, Sagittarius people tend to have a little bit of everything in their closet,” Bond says. “They likely have very different clothes for different occasions.” However, don’t be fooled by their versatility. Bond explains that this fire sign can still be expected to “make a statement and catch the eye.” They love to show off their personal tastes, whether that’s with bold prints or a retro silhouette. 

    But no matter their signature look, a Sagittarius is a certified trendsetter. “As charismatic leaders, it’s not uncommon to see a Sagittarius defining the trends of the day,” Bond shares. “As a fire sign, they rock it first – and with total confidence (Christina Aguilera and Nicki Minaj both have a huge Sagittarius presence in their charts). They’re drawn to the bold, the flashy and blingy, and the sexy.”

    Although this sign has a flair for the dramatic, they still value their comfort. Bond points out that you won’t find a Sagittarius wearing an outfit that “compromises their ability to move around” or has too many details. Looking for some examples? Ahead, we’ve rounded up 10 ways to dress like a Sagittarius.

    Meet the expert

    Taryn Bond is an evolutionary-intuitive astrologer with over 129,000 followers on TikTok. 

    Sleek Suits

    A Sagittarius loves a power suit moment. “Suits are a wonderful option for Sagittarius placements,” Bond says. “They can sometimes be deceptively comfortable but will still give the authority with which Sagittarius intends to greet the world.” Even if you prefer neutral tones, you can still make a statement with this style by opting for no undershirt for a bold yet classic look.

    Shop Similar: Alice + Olivia Breann Long Fitted Blazer, $495, and Alice + Olivia Stacey Slim Trouser, $245

    Loafers

    According to Bond, Sagittarius is ruled by Jupiter, which is the planet of wisdom and philosophy. This leads the sign to take “inspiration from vintage ivy-professor or old English writer energy.” Enter a classic shoe choice: loafers. “The sophistication and practicality of the loafer is an irresistible combination for Sag — it matches their lifestyle and essence all too well,” she explains.

    Shop Similar:     Coach Leah Loafer, $195

    Silk Fabrics

    “Silk and similar textiles are a perfect match for Sagittarius’s elegance, adding that little bit of ‘extra’ that the sign is known for,” says Bond. Sagittarius is also characterized by style versatility, so she points out they tend to wear this look in one of three ways. “We may see a Sagittarius rocking an elegant silk maxidress or dressing down a midi skirt with a beat-up tee and sneakers,” Bond explains. Those who prefer a more structured look may opt for a silk blouse paired with jeans or tailored pants.

    Shop Similar: Reformation Jeany Silk Dress, $328

    Flowy Pants

    While this sign is all about eye-catching looks, not every Sagittarius will reach for a form-fitting garment. “Sometimes a Sagittarius just cannot do tight pants,” Bond says. “You’ll likely see a Sagittarius pair flowy, non-restrictive pants with a cropped sweater or dressed up with a blazer.” Even with a more relaxed silhouette, a Sagittarius can still be bold with this style by choosing a pair of flowy pants in a vibrant hue or pattern. 

    Shop Similar: L’Agence Lillian Wide Leg Pants, $395

    Patterned Tights

    More is more when it comes to a Sagittarius’s accessories. They’re not afraid to experiment with items that draw more attention to their outfit, which includes patterned tights. Bond says, “They may go for a gentle polka dot for professional looks and something more interesting — perhaps even bedazzled — for a night out.” While on more casual days, “we might find Sag rocking a pair of ripped-up tights with combat boots and an oversized tee.”

    Shop Similar: Calzedonia Tartan 50 Denier Mesh Eco Tights, $15

    Intricate Bralettes

    As a fire sign, Bond says it’s natural for a Sagittarius to opt for something eye-catching and perhaps a bit revealing. Though bralettes are typically layered under a top, a Sagittarius takes a bolder approach. “Pairing a blazer or flowy set with a beautiful bralette is only natural for Sagittarius: They want to send a message, but they’ve also got somewhere to be,” Bond shares. “A unique lacy bralette with a floral print or sparkles is the perfect leave-'em-wanting-more Sagittarian move.”

    Shop Similar: By Anthropologie Seamless Lace-Trim Bralette, $28

    Sheath Dresses

    “Although a Sagittarius’s reputation as a fun-loving free-spirit is undeniable in many natives of the sign, their elegance and grace must not be forgotten,” says Bond, adding that “a layered mesh sheath dress is a breathtaking complement to a Sag’s physique while exuding a vibe of class, sophistication, and celebration.” Try styling a sheath dress with statement jewelry, such as layered chain necklaces or drop hoop earrings.

    Shop Similar: Steve Madden Izzo Midi Dress, $119

    Baseball Hats

    Sagittarius season may not coincide with baseball season, but this fire sign will still represent their favorite team with their accessories. “As a mutable fire sign, Sagittarius is the king or queen of ‘just rolled out of bed’ messy hair,” Bond says. “ Luckily, baseball hats totally balance their desire to look good while sometimes simply not having the time to put in total effort every morning.” If you’re not a sports fan, we recommend you opt for a baseball cap in a solid hue that will go with any outfit in your wardrobe.

    Shop Similar: ‘47 NY Baseball Cap, $42

    Satchel Bag

    Another wardrobe staple beloved by a Sagittarius is a satchel bag. “A buckle satchel is a common go-to for visionary Sagittarius,” says Bond. “Its ease of use, spaciousness, and elegance speak to the sign’s core motivations.” But even with such a timeless accessory, some Sagittarians can’t help their adventurous nature. According to Bond, “If they go for a small satchel, Sagittarians tend to be drawn towards embellished or embroidered purses.”

    Shop Similar: Kate Spade Gramercy Medium Satchel, $314 (Originally $448)

    Oversized Shawl

    Sagittarius season marks the last month of fall, so many with this sign will look for garments to keep them warm but still appeal to their adventurous spirit. “Oversized shawls or scarves that can wrap around the body give a chic, classy look that also appeals to the fire sign’s flair for the dramatic,” explains Bond. “Auburn, deep purples, and burnt orange tones are especially powerful here. These shades are associated with Sagittarius and allows them to still have a big presence even when cold weather limits some fashion choices.” 

    Shop Similar: Rosie Sugden Gauzy Cashmere Wrap, $285
