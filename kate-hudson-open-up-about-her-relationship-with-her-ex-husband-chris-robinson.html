    Kate Hudson recently featured on Sara Foster and Erin Foster’s podcast, The World’s First Podcast. During her appearance on the program, she discussed her relationship with her ex-husband, Chris Robinson. Robinson, according to Hudson, showed her what it was like to be “unconditionally loved.” She went on to say that she and Robinson first met when she was 20 years old in New York. Kate said that she married him when she was 21 years old, and she went on to say:

    “I basically throw myself into whatever I do. People felt it was rash, but I simply said, ‘You know what? I’m simply going to get right in. I’m not going to second-guess myself. I’m madly in love with him. I’m not going to pretend that we should wait.”

    Kate Hudson revealed that she was in love with Chris Robinson and had no second thoughts about marrying him. She said that she and Chris “worshipped” and were in love. Hudson went on to say that Robinson showed her what it was like to be “unconditionally loved.”

    According to Fox News, Hudson also stated:

    “He was also essential in teaching me what it meant to be unconditionally loved. Chris truly unlocked the floodgates for me…forever. No matter how hard the relationship became at one time, meeting him and him enabling me to comprehend what it felt like was the most crucial moment for me.”

    In August 2006, Kate Hudson and Chris Robinson divorced.

    Kate Hudson and Chris Robinson met at a New York party in 2000 and started living together a few days later. In December 2000, the couple married in Aspen, Colorado. Ryder, their son, was born in January 2004 and the couple quickly became parents. According to E! News, they split in August 2006, and Chris filed for divorce in November of that year, claiming irreconcilable differences.

    Chris sought joint custody of Ryder, which was granted in October 2007 when the divorce was completed. However, in February 2017, he filed a petition for a re-evaluation of the custody arrangement. An evaluator was engaged to talk with the former spouse and their kid in order to reach a conclusion and assist in the development of a parenting plan that included legal and physical custody.

    According to E! News, Hudson was forced to pay $7,500 as an assessment fee. However, public documents did not explain why Chris requested a re-evaluation or what he intended to do. More information concerning the duo’s son’s custody is anticipated. Kate Hudson has appeared in films such as Fool’s Gold, Bride Wars, The Skeleton Key, Nine, Glass Onion, Mother’s Day, and others. Chris Robinson, on the other hand, is a Black Crowes member. He’s also released solo material such as New Earth Mud, Bootleg, This Magnificent Distance, and others.
