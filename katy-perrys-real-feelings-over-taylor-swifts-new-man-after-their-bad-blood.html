    Taylor Swift has a new beau, and Katy Perry has expressed how she feels about Tay Tay’s new romance.

    It isn’t a secret pop music stars often get wrapped in ‘dramas’ and sometimes it can last several years. Lucky for Taylor Swift and Katy Perry fans, they appear to have overcome theirs. The iconic celebrity feud officially ‘ended’ when Katy showed up for Taylor’s You Need To Calm Down video dressed as a burger to Taylor’s french fries (the perfect combination). Years after the official end of their fight, the Fireworks hitmaker is opening up about how she feels about Travis Kelce, Taylor’s dreamy new beau!

    Katy Perry’s real feelings over Taylor Swift’s new beau

    Singer and songwriter Katy Perry is as excited about ‘Traylor’ (celebrity couple nickname for Taylor and Travis) as Swifties are, apparently.

    The rising frenzy over the hottest couple on the block has reached the celebrity realm. The Hot N Cold crooner left a positive comment on a post talking about Taylor’s new romance.

    The 38-year-old music legend commented: “I ship it” on a Traylor photo, letting fans know her true feelings about the newly formed couple.

    Katy Perry joins the likes of other stars who have also come out in support of Taylor’s new ‘relationship’. This includes Sophie Turner, Blake Lively, and her husband Ryan Reynolds, as they were seen attending the Kansas City Chiefs’ match together with Tay Tay.

    Taylor Swift and Katy Perry’s ‘Bad Blood’ drama

    Taylor Swift and Katy Perry’s feud was not about any ‘guy’ but simply about ‘business.’ For years, the duo did not address the feud publicly, but Swift revealed she wrote Bad Blood about an unnamed singer with whom she was “straight-up enemies.”

    The 33-year-old Grammy-award-winning artist claimed she didn’t know where she stood with the California Girls hitmaker.

    Speaking of a so-called sabotage, she added at the time: “And it wasn’t even about a guy! It had to do with business. She basically tried to sabotage an entire arena tour. She tried to hire a bunch of people out from under me. And I’m surprisingly non-confrontational—you would not believe how much I hate conflict. So, now I have to avoid her. It’s awkward, and I don’t like it.”

    Later, Katy Perry addressed the feud in a talk show in 2017. She said: “Honestly, it’s really like she started it, and it’s time for her to finish it.”

    The duo finally ended their fight in 2019 when Taylor invited Katy to feature in her music video, You Need To Calm Down. It looks like they both have, and now Katy is a supportive friend.
